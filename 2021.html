Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

​
Jaya Srila Gurudeva!

Welcome! This website is for you to submit your Vyāsa-pūjā offering to Śrila Gurudeva, Parama-pujyapāda Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyaṇa Goswāmī Mahārāja, for the glorious upcoming event – the 100th anniversary of his divine appearance. You are welcome to submit a written and/or spoken (in a video format) homage that will be presented here online, beginning from the day of his Centennial appearance day, February 11, 2021.

This online version will help us gather and publish offerings in different languages from devotees all over the world. We hope your offering will become a special part in this fragrant bouquet of loving offerings, we will collectively offer to Śrila Gurudeva on his divine Centennial appearance day. We are also confident that your personal heartfelt expression of love will reach him and will please him very much.

Team Centennial is bringing the Vyāsa-pūjā book online so that not only will everyone be able to see it on Śrila Gurudeva’s Centennial Vyāsa-pūjā day, but generations of devotees will have the benefit of reading the Vaisnava glorification for years to come. Everyone will be able to read, hear and remember Srila Gurudeva's glory, thus strengthening our transcendental relationship with him.

If you face any problem registering or submitting your offering, please Contact us.

You can also email your offering to sgvp2021@gmail.com or by WhatsApp to +1 281-650-8235.
