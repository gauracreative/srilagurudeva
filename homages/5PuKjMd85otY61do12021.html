Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Hare Krishna Das

India, Vrindavan

vande ’ham śrī guru varaṁ
śrī rūpānuga pravaram
vraja-rasa rasikaṁ ca
nārāyaṇaṁ taṁ prapannam

I adore the greatest of divine masters, Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyaṇa Gosvāmī Mahārāja; he is the foremost follower of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, and is always relishing vraja-rasa—I surrender unto him.

śrī guru caranaṁ vande
ramaṇa preṣṭhāya bhūtale
rūpānuga bhaktidaṁ ca kṛpa
mūrti nārāyaṇam

I bow down to the lotus feet of the embodiment of mercy, Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyaṇa Gosvāmī Mahārāja, who is dearly loved by Śrī Ramaṇa-bihārī, and who gives the treasure of rūpānuga bhakti.

sevā-kunje vraje ramye
govardhana girau sadā
rādhā-kuṇḍe rasānande
tat tat sevā pradāyakam

Śrīla Gurudeva is eternally residing in the beautiful Sevā-kunja of Vraja, at Giri Govardhana, and at Rādhā-kuṇḍa. there, he is immersed in the bliss of nectarean mellows or rasa, and he grants entrance into the service of Rādhā-Kṛṣṇa as their maidservant.

guruṁ nārāyanākhyaṁ tam
vande vinoda preṣṭhakam
yat pāda smṛti mātrena
dāmodara prasīdati

I pray to Śrīla Gurudeva, Bhaktivedānta Nārāyaṇa Gosvāmī Mahārāja, who is very dear to Śrī Vinoda Manjarī. Simply by remembering his lotus feet one pleases Śrī Śrī Rādhā-Dāmodara.

nāma-śreṣṭhaṁ manum api śacī-putram atra svarūpaṁ
rūpaṁ tasyāgrajam uru-purīṁ māthurīṁ goṣṭhavāṭīm
rādhā-kuṇḍaṁ giri-varam aho! rādhikā-mādhavāśāṁ
prāpto yasya prathita-kṛpayā śrī guruṁ taṁ nato ’smi

I offer praṇāma to Srī Guru, by whose abundant mercy I have obtained the topmost name of Śrī Hari, the dīkṣā-mantras, [the service of] Śrī Śacīnandana Gaurahari, Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara, Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī, his older brother Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī, the vast abode of Śrī Mathurā, Vraja, Rādhā-kuṇḍa, and Govardhana, as well as a desire for [the loving service of] Śrī Śrī Rādhā Mādhava.

Dear Srila Gurudeva, in this most auspicious occasion of your transcendental appearance, I want to thank you for coming to the west. Many countless times I lamented that I had never met Srila Prabhupada, however by his causeless mercy I was able to come into your presence.

As I travelled first to the US from Bolivia with the desire to take the shelter of Srila Bhakti Pramoda Puri Gosvami Maharaja I had the immense blessing of coming in contact with you and your dear associates. At that time after meeting your effulgent brahmacaris and hearing their katha I was wonderstruck and thought to myself “If the disciples are so qualified and filled with so much love how amazing must their guru be!”

Then very quickly after some days of hearing your nectarean katha my heart was placed in an awkward position. I revealed to you that I was having the desire to travel to India to take initiation from Srila Bhakti Pramoda Puri Gosvami Maharaja, but upon seeing you my heart had been stolen as I had never met a pure devotee of Srila Prabhupada’s calibre in my life. At that time you revealed to me that you and he (Srila Puri Gosvāmī Maharaja) were non-different. It was then by your causeless mercy that you gave me shelter at your lotus feet. Just then and there you asked me what my name was and I told you my friends and family called me Hari. You then said “Hari..Hare..Hare Krishna. Hare Krishna Dasa is a good name for you." At that moment I was a bit shocked as Hare Krishnas did not have such a good name in my country and I was hoping that my diksa name wouldn’t be so obvious. Now definitely can’t hide that I am a Hare Krishna! Then you told me “Do you know what Hare Krishna means? I then said “It means the names of Radha and Krishna right?” Then you said “Hare Krishna is the name of Caitanya Mahaprabhu. He is the combined form of Sri Radha and Krishna and He has given His name for our deliverance.”

Since then by your causeless mercy unlimited fortune has come in my life. I told you at that time that I had a deep desire to be able to go and stay with you in India to which you mercifully gave consent.

Again you made all the arrangements that I could get there. Once in Mathura you placed me under the shelter of your dear Premananda Prabhuji who was the mother of Sri Kesavaji Gaudiya Matha.

Experiencing kartika Vraja-mandala parikrama with you and Srila Bhaktivedanta Trivikrama Gosvami Maharaja in 1999 was an unforgettable experience. You gave us so much love and infused in all deep appreciation and relationship with Srimati Radhika’s and Sri Syamasundara’s holy dhama.

You taught us in Govardhana that Giriraja can fulfil all and any desires material and spiritual, but we should not be cheated. You said we should only recite this prayer:

śyāmasundara śikhaṇḍa-śekhara
smera-hāsa muralī-manohara
rādhikā-rasika māḿ kṛpā-nidhe
sva-priyā-caraṇa-kińkarīḿ kuru
–Sri Radha-Prarthana (Verse 2)

"O Syamasundara! O You whose head is adorned with peacock feathers! Your face is always graced with a playful smile, Your flute-playing is enchanting, and You are expert in relishing rasa with Srimati Radhika. Because You are an ocean of mercy, I am appealing to You to please make me a kinkari (maidservant) at the feet of Your beloved."

You told us that our objective in life was summoned in this verse. Again and again you had seemingly quarrelling discussions with Srila Trivikrama Gosvami Maharaja where you would establish Srimati Radhika’s superiority over Krishna and glorify Sri Giriraja as “hari-dasa-varya” the greatest of all of Hari’s servants who assisted Sri Radha and Krishna in their most intimate pastimes.

I recall how Srila Trivikrama Gosvami Maharaja once took the microphone and didn’t let you speak. Most of your disciples were upset. Then later we came to know that the doctor had forbidden you to speak for long and Srila Maharaja out of his deep love and concern for your health had acted in that manner.

I also remember that year how you would walk so fast and lead us. Even at your advanced age we couldn’t catch up with you. Those parikramas were very intimate we all slept in the hall just next to you in Modi-bhavana in Govardhana and Varsana also.

You mercifully gave us darsana of all the holy places through your direct vision of your eternal home. You were not simply reciting something you had read or heard, but revealed us about your eternal abode where you are the intimate associate of Srimati Radhika and Krishna.

You said the only price is “greed”. And how to get this greed? We can only attain it by listening to the nectarean harikatha from your lotus lips.

Gurudeva, how can we ever forget your loving exchanges with all the vrajavasis? The people in Mathura loved you so much. You very sweetly invited all the pandits and smashed all their ideas that Krishna was born in Mathura. You completely transformed such a tough city by your sweetness.

You fulfilled Parama Gurudeva’s desire of establishing the super-excellence of Vraja above the opulence of Mathura and Dvaraka.

As Parama Gurudeva named you, you are really “Bhakta-bandhava” the friend of all devotees. Those festivals you organized in Mathura for Janmastami and Radhastami were beyond words. We would serve prasadam all day non-stop. You would have a great parade all over Mathura with elephants and kirtana. Unforgettable times.

Gurudeva, thank you for your kindness that you always have arranged shelter for us near your dearest. Thank you for always taking care of us and providing constant sadhu-sanga. Thank you for allowing me to continue to be near the vaisnavas who are always reminding me of you and teaching me the process of bhakti so that also one day I can become a real disciple.

I will never be able to repay you. But I beg you to continue keeping me under constant sadhu-sanga so that one day I may also be able to serve your lotus feet.

Please let me develop internally the mood of Vraja and be able to reside there under your lotus feet.

Your aspiring disciple

Hare Krishna Dasa
