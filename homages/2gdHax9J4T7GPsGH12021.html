Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Brajesh Das

India, New Delhi

First, I offer my humble obeisances at the glorious lotus feet of my beloved Gurudeva nitya lila pravishta Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja. On the most auspicious special day of the 100th birth anniversary of my beloved Gurudeva I feel very loved and humbled to offer my heartfelt pushpanjali at His glorious lotus feet.

I met Srila Gurudeva in the year 1991 with my elder brother Vishnu Prabhu, my parents and pujyapad Ramchandra prabhuji in Vrindavan. He was sitting on a chair and Pujyapad Madhav Maharaja ji (at that time Naveen Krishna Bramchari ) was standing behind him. It was, I think, the evening time. When my parents saw him they made up their mind that they want to take initiation from Srila Gurudeva. I was very young in age at that time. My parents invited Srila Gurudeva to visit our home in Delhi and he accepted the invitation.

In the year 1991 summers, Srila Gurudeva visited our house for the first time and it was a memorable experience for all of us. So many devotees started coming to our house. We used to have so many house programmes. Starting from the morning till the evening, the entire day used to remain busy in the service of Gurudeva. My mother used to be busy in making prasadam. I used to do Srila Gurudeva body massage and then in the evening He used to speak harikatha. So many people used to come. Many were invited from our neighbourhood also.

In 1991 when my parents took initiation, I took harinama from Srila Gurudeva and in 1999 I also took initiation from Him. He gave me the name Brajesh das. That day was the last day of Kartik month. We were in Roop Sanathan Gaudiya math. When the morning class was going on, I was preparing myself for the initiation ceremony. I got my head shaved off for the first time and no hesitation was there in mind. After the class was over the announcement was made that all those devotees who were taking Brahman diksha should get ready and come to Gopinath bhavan. Then I, along with some more devotees, went to Gopinath bhavan. My mother came with me there. Srila Gurudeva was in his room on the first floor and we stood in queue on the stairs and waited for the Gurudeva’s call. Then after some time he called up all the devotees and we sat in his room. He handed over the mantras written on a piece of paper and explained the meaning to all of us. He was very happy to see me taking initiation. We were two boys from Delhi. After Gurudeva finished the class we were told to go to Roop Sanatan temple for the fire ceremony. Then we all offered our dandavat pranamas to Srila Gurudeva and went to the temple. Then fire ceremony was performed. After the ceremony, I was first in the line standing and Gurudeva put the Janehu. After that we started bhiksha from the devotees present there and offered to Srila Gurudeva. It was really a very transforming experience for me.

Now, after many years of association with Srila Gurudeva and after hearing so much harikatha from his sweet voice I have understood the important teachings he gave for sadhakas to make spiritual progress.

1. What is the goal of our human life:- As Srila Gurudeva always explained that we must have the goal of our life in bhakti sadhana then only we can achieve something. Our goal should not be only to earn money and engage ourselves in material things but to devote our life in Radha Krishna's divine service and Guru & Vaishnav seva. Then by the mercy of Hari, Guru & Vaisnavas we can understand the tattva-siddhanta. Otherwise it is very difficult. So the goal should be to chant the Lord's name with sincerity, to serve Hari, Guru and Vaisnavas and to preach Gurudeva’s mission.

2. Why chanting is important:- In our day to day life we come across so many challenges . These challenges can be positive or negative. Positive challenges are motivating but negative challenges are very hard and stressful. To overcome the stress in life we should do more chanting. Lord has given his entire sweetness in his Holy Name and if we chant it with sincerity then we can handle any kind of stress in life. While chanting we should focus on each syllable properly. We should hear it with our own ears what we are chanting. Then only we can concentrate. We should also focus on the sweet pastimes of Sri Radha Krishna while chanting. Then only our chanting will be fruitful. Otherwise our mind is very sensitive. It runs here and there very fast. All types of disturbances start coming to the mind when we start chanting. This is the problem. The chanting is very very important. The more we chant the more we can control our mind otherwise it is very difficult to control the mind. Our Gurudeva always used to say that not even a one day of your life should pass without chanting harinama. So as a devotee we must make it a principle that we have to chant regularly with sincerity and complete our counts of 16 rounds everyday.

3.Reading books: - This is a very important part. As our Gurudeva has always said that we should always read our shastras. If we don’t read the books we will not be able to learn about the tattva-gyan, about our Guru parampara, about Gaurang Mahaprabhu, about the beautiful lilas of Sri Radha Krishna, about the holy places in Braja dham, about the teachings of our Guru parampara. So reading is very important. Once we read then we can preach Mahaprabhu's mission and Srila Gurudeva's teachings. We can understand the Sanatan dharma.

4. Doing Kirtan: - Our Gurudeva used to tell us that we must do kirtan everyday. We must learn bhajans and then we should sing the bhajans with our bhava/mood. I am very fond of kirtan. During the early years of my association with Gurudeva I used to see Krishna Das prabhuji that how beautifully he performed the kirtan. I have learned a lot watching him and other devotees doing kirtan. I used to play Mridangam during our house programmes and sometimes sing kirtan also. I got the chances to play mrindagam with devotees in Mathura and Vrindavan.

5. Listening to Harikatha:- One of the most important aspect I have learned is to listen harikatha. I really feel blessed that our Guru-varga have given us so beautiful tattva-siddhanta to understand in our life. If these siddhantas were not there we as humans would have made our life miserable like animals because in material life everything seems stressful. The relationships are uncertain, the human nature is becoming self-centered. So when we listen harikatha, we listen about the beautiful pastimes of Sri Radha Krishna, about Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, about the beautiful teachings which our Guru parampara has given us through their valuable writings, all of these enlighten us. They are really precious. In Hindi we call (Anmol rattan ). So at least in our daily routine I am trying my best to hear the harikatha and try to focus on such teachings. In our human life if we spend our valuable time on such things its very beneficial for us.

After Gurudeva disappeared from this world in the year 2010, it was very painful. Very hard to believe. Initially whenever Gurudeva used to visit Delhi everyday used to be a festival kind of the day. But now it seems different. During Kartik parikrama lakhs and lakhs of devotees from all over the world used to visit Braja dham and do parikrama. So many years passed like watching and taking part in such beautiful parikramas. My heart felt so happy to see the love among devotees. Our Gurudeva has always taught us that we must respect each other and shower our love for each other. We must all be united for the happiness of our Gurudeva. We must all love each other as we all are the sons and daughters of our beloved Gurudeva. We are one family of Gaudiya devotees.

In the end I really feel blessed about myself, about my life that I am a disciple of Srila Gurudeva who is none other than Sri Raman Manjari of Srimati Radha Rani. I feel blessed about myself that I got the beautiful opportunity to serve my beloved Gurudeva at our home, to perform kirtan in front of Him, to do his body massage, to serve prasadam to Him, to take Him to different places in Delhi for house programmes whenever got the chance, to bring Him home from airport when He used to come from abroad preaching in my own car and fortunate to become his driver. I feel blessed that I am born in a devoted family. God has gifted us with talented and devoted children, with devoted wife and with loving parents and loving siblings. I feel blessed that my Gurudeva used to stay in my room and my room is very fortunate to serve Him. I feel blessed with those beautiful memories in my heart with the beautiful association of our beloved Gurudeva. I pray to His lotus feet that He showers His mercy upon me and I groom up myself to preach His beautiful mission of love and affection of Radha Krishna prema Bhakti so that I can make my life valuable and can add some meaning to it. Oh! My beloved Gurudeva, please keep showering your mercy on this fallen soul who is begging for your mercy and aspire to do your loving seva in the form of kirtan, katha and distribution. I hope one day you will feel proud about me. Thanks a lot in abundance, Gurudeva for giving me your love, your affection, your association to a conditioned soul like me and for making my human birth successful.

Aspiring to be in your service always

Brajesh Das
