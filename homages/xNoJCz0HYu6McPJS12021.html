Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Dhirkrishna das

India, Bangalore

vande 'ham sri guru varam sri rupanuga pravaram
vraja-rasa-rasikam ca narayanam tam prapanam

All glory to Sri Guru and Gauranga!

I was not so sure - how can I ever glorify my guru-padapadma? Especially when I could not touch even a tiny bit of his endless glories, his transcendental mood of service, what treasure of pure bhakti he came to inject in our hearts! Still, I have heard from my guru-padapadma:

mukam karoti vacalam
pangum langhayate girim
yat-kripa tam aham vande
sri-gurum dina-taranam
–From Sri Caitanya-Caritamrta by Srila Krsnadasa Kaviraja Gosvami, Madhya-Lila 17.80

The mercy of a sad-guru turns the dumb into eloquent speakers and enables the lame to cross mountains. So, by his mercy, I am trying to pen down some of my memories with Srila Gurudeva.

I was in touch with ISKCON devotees since my school days. After schooling, I came to Bangalore for higher education and used to visit Hare Krsna Hills (ISKCON Bangalore) regularly. I was reading His Divine Grace Srila AC Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada’s books and developed nistha at his lotus feet and used to feel Srila Prabhupada is everything for me.

One day in temple a devotee said to me that dancing on kirtan is fine but it is important to take shelter of a sad-guru to begin bhakti. I used to pray to Prabhupada to send a bonafide spiritual master to me. There was confusion in my mind as Bangalore ISKCON used to follow the ritvik process and accept only Srila Prabhupada as spiritual master by listening to diksa mantras from his recorded audios.

After a few weeks, my relative Sriman Radhesh Prabhuji called me and advised me to visit the preaching center of Srila Gurudeva's sanga that was newly started in Bangalore City. Per his advice, I went to the Basveshawara Nagar Centre and started associating with Sripad Bhaktivedanta Vaikhanas Maharaja and other brahmacaris. A few days later I got the news that Srila Gurudeva is coming!

I had been to Vrndavana earlier and associated with Pujyapad Bhaktivedanta Sridhara Maharaja (back in those days, Sripad Rasananda Prabhu) during my college days and had heard about Srila Gurudeva from him. I was extremely delighted that Prabhupada listened to my prayers! I started wondering if I can get harinama initiation from Srila Gurudeva.

Along with the devotees, I went to the airport to receive Srila Gurudeva and then we all headed to the Sri Ranganatha Gaudiya Matha at Hesaraghatta. After the program in the evening, I was standing outside Gurudeva's room to have a darshan before leaving, feeling a bit scared and nervous. Srila Gurudeva saw me and called me inside. I paid obeisance at his lotus feet. Gurudeva asked for my name, where I am from, etc.

Sripad Bhaktivedanta Madhava Maharaja told me, "Radhesh has requested to give you diksa." I requested to take harinama initiation first, follow the all rules and regulations strictly, and the take diksa mantras in Vrndavana when I come for parikrama. I was joyful and all my nervousness was overcome by the warmth of Srila Gurudeva. Then the day came when Gurudeva would mercifully give me harinama.

Then one strange thing happened. Gurudeva asked, “Your brother didn’t come? He was supposed to come, right?”

I was wondering how Gurudeva knew this! My brother was in Hyderabad, he was planning to come but didn't inform anyone. Only my brother and I knew about this plan, so how did Gurudeva know….?

There was another lila of Gurudeva I remember well. Gurudeva was antaryami - he could see past, present and future. When I received harinama initiation there was another devotee with me. To the other devotee, he said, “Don’t get married; your life will be destroyed if you do.”

Afterwards, all senior devotees advised this devotee to follow Gurudeva’s words. But somehow he could not and got married. Slowly he left Vaisnava association and then completely went away from Srila Gurudeva's sanga. Then I understood what Srila Gurudeva meant when he said to him not to get married.

Gurudeva gave me some instructions to live a humble devotional life. I could not comprehend much at that time, but I know my Gurudeva is always guiding me. When I used to go for parikrama and sit in the temple hall to listen to Harikatha from his lotus mouth, like everyone says, I also used to feel that Gurudeva loves me and he is looking at me. Although I could meet him only once a year, I never felt so. It actually felt as if Srila Gurudeva was there always and he had so much affection for me!

Glories of my guru-padapadma are endless! O Srila Gurudeva, you are vibhu and I am anu (an atom)! My sthula-buddhi could not understand your glories! I sincerely pray at your lotus feet that one day by your mercy, I will be able to practice the path of pure bhakti!

vancha-kalpatarubhyas ca kripa-sindhubhya eva ca
patitanam pavanebhyo vaishnavebhyo namo namah

Dhirkrishna Das
