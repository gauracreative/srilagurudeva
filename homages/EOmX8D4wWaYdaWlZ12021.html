Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Sripad B.V. Bishnu Maharaja

India, Orissa

Tridandi Bikshu Bhaktivichar Bishnu Maharaja (left) | Srila Gurudeva

Centennial Vyasa-puja of H.D.G. Nityalila Pravistha Om Vishnupada 108 Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayan Goswami Maharaja

First Meeting

It was in the year 1979 when I was traveling to Mathura with my guru H.D.G. Bhaktivaibhava Puri Goswami Maharaj, that I was blessed to meet H. D. G. Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayan Goswami Maharaj for the first time, at Keshavji Gaudiya Math. I was a witness to their relationship on that day and I could understand how close and intimate a connection Srila Maharaja had with my Guru Maharaj. Present also at that time were Sripad Premananda Prabhu, Sripad Subhananda Prabhu (late B.V Teerth Maharaj) Sripad Naveen Prabhu (Sripad B.V. Madhav Maharaj), and other Vaishnavas that were so sincere and immersed in their exemplary service mood to Srila B. V. Narayan Goswami Maharaj. I was deeply impressed by their association.

His Relationship With Us - Temple Inaugurations

On many occasions we were fortunate that Srila B. V. Narayana Goswami Maharaja gracefully accepted our invitation to attend temple openings and deity installations. In the year 1988, Srila Maharaj came to Bhaktivinod Ashram (Anand Nagar, Brahmapur, Odisha) for the inauguration of the newly constructed temple and deity installation function, with all the Math brahmacharis. And in 1982, during the Ratha Yatra festival time, he came to inaugurate the temple and install deities at Sri Chaitanya Chandra Ashram (Aitota, Puri, Odisha). Again, in 1984 he came to inaugurate the temple and install deities at Kovur, (West Godavari, A.P.) In the year 1989 Srila Maharaj came to inaugurate the temple and install deities at Lawson's Bay Colony, Radha Nagar, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Many other festival occasions, when our Guru Maharaj would extend an invitation, we would feel so happy when His Divine Grace would come and grace our temple functions with his divine presence.

Vraja Mandal Parikrama

By the mercy of my Gurudev, I was traveling from Odisha to Vrindavan for Braja Mandal Parikrama in the company of hundreds of Oriya devotees during the month of Kartik. At that time, I was fortunate to join the parikrama group under the expert guidance of Srila B. V. Narayan Goswami Maharaj. Eagerly hearing from him during Vraja Mandal parikrama, I was able to know many divine pastimes of Sri Sri Radha Govinda. Srila Maharaj was a top class rasik Vaishnava, so when he would explain the Lord’s divine pastimes, immediately he would become ecstatic, full of divine emotion (bhava).

Sri Vraja Mandal Parikrama

Once during parikrama, he was explaining in Hindi and on his request, I was explaining in Odia language. Many secret pastimes were discussed, and it was my extreme good fortune that Maharaj praised and loved me in our hari-katha. In lila-katha he was relating to me as I was on Krsna's side and he was considering himself to be on Radharani's side. So, many times, he was punching me, enacting the angry mood of Radharani and Gopi Bhava. Once in ''Belwaban”, he was narrating the glories of Rasa-lila where the Goddess of Fortune, Laxmi Devi came out from Vaikuntha to enjoy the Rasa dance. This was all to enlighten us with the glory of Vraja Dham. He also explained how Lord Siva became Gopiswar but could not enter Rasa-lila. Maharaj was explaining in great detail, quoting many verses from Bhagavatam and Goswami scriptures. After he finished speaking, he ordered me to speak in the Odia language. Many Odisha devotees were present in the Parikrama. I repeated Maharaj's Harikatha, just as I had heard it, and I sang one song from Gita Govinda where Srila Jayadev Goswami was explaining Rasa-lila. After finishing the Odia Harikatha, Maharaj was so happy, he embraced me. He said, “Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu sent devotees from Nilachala to join with our Parikrama”. In this way, many times we were blessed to enjoy Lila-katha with Srila B. V. Narayana Goswami Maharaja in our affectionate relationship during Parikrama time.

During the year of Sri Rupa Sanatan Goudiya Math’s auspicious inauguration there was a special parikrama, and I was feeling ecstatic in the association of my sannyasa guru H. D. G. Bhakti Jivan Janardana Goswami Maharaj, H. D. G. Bhakti Vedanta Tribikram Maharaj, H. D. G. Bhakti Vedanta Paryatak Maharaj, and many other great sannyasis and brahmacharis of Gaudiya Vedanta Samiti. In such excellent Vaishnava association, continuously hearing Hari-katha on different topics, there was an extraordinarily attractive, auspicious atmosphere generated. H. D. G. Bhakti Vedanta Tribikram Maharaj's devotional mood was deeply absorbed in tasting different rasas. So, it is difficult to properly explain here in so many words. Such deep realizations were spoken. I was so fortunate to get such blessings from great rasik Vaishnava association.

Srila B. V. Narayan Goswami Maharaj was a very strict follower of all the principles of Vaishnav behavior of our Gaudiya sampradaya. Everyday without fail, he was following his daily (sadhana) practice. I personally witnessed how he was teaching the brahmacharies to practice and understand our scriptures. Every day he was encouraging them, explaining verses from many scriptures and tattva from different angles of vision. As an ideal Acharya, he was always teaching his followers to maintain purity, and he practiced it himself without fail. I also experienced in Nabadvip parikrama and Vrajamandal parikrama how he was leading all devotees by his personal example, how to maintain our standing in spiritual life without any difficulties.

Srila Maharaj possessed all divine qualities of an empowered Acharya. Achara and prachar was the example he showed, up to the last breath.

Even at the advanced age of 80 he traveled to preach abroad. Continuously, and tirelessly, he distributed his Rasa Katha for the benefit of vaishnavas all over the world. He attracted many experienced devotees, and new recruits also, and encouraged everyone to comprehend the higher realms of Gaudiya siddhanta, which is necessary for our spiritual progress. He published many of the Goswami's literature in English, Hindi, Spanish, Russian, and several other languages. He also distributed the incomparable wealth of Vraja prem and Raganuga bhakti to western devotees without discrimination.

In this Kali-yuga the whole world is suffering in different ways, both physically and mentally. No one is peaceful. Financially, the world has become dependent on world of techno-science. Everyone has a smartphone and internet and are busily engaged with many kinds of media connections. But the naked truth is that in spite of technological advancement, human beings are internally empty, frustrated, and suffering in many ways. They desperately need Krsna bhakti rasa, Harikatha and Harinam sankirtan, now, more than ever. The Yuga Dharma is Sri Krsna's Harinam sankirtan, our motto is, “param vijayate Sri Krishna sankirtanam”. But without the presence of such great Vaishnavas as His Divine Grace it is very difficult to preach in this Kali-yuga. Now, many such great Acharyas have left this world, leaving it a darker, more sinful place. And the future will be darker by the influence of Kali-yuga. Still, there is all hope for spiritual progress if we always remember, and follow in the footsteps of such a great, pure Rasika Vaishnav like His Divine Grace Srila B. V. Narayan Goswami Maharaj. We pray that he showers his mercy on us all from the spiritual abode.

On the occasion of the Centennial Vyasa-puja festival of Srila Maharaj I am thankful to Sripad Bhaktivedanta Bodhayan Maharaj[previously Vamsi-vadan prabhu]who offered me this small service to remember the glorious life of Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Goswami Maharaja, and to recall my cherished memories of him.

In the service of Hari, Guru, and Vaishnavas,
Tridandi Bikshu Bhaktivichar Bishnu Maharaj

(Acharya – Sri Krishna Chaitanya Mission)
