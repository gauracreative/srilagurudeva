Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Manju Dasi

Germany, Stuttgart

Dear Srila Gurudeva,

To You I bow down

Please accept my humble obeisances at your lotus feet. Please accept this inept attempt to express my appreciation and everlasting gratitude.

On my endless journey through countless universes and a myriad of lives, I suddenly stood directly in front of You in the narrow back entrance of Sri Rupa Sanatana Gaudiya Math. I was a young mother, my husband next to me and our baby in my arms.

Your sapphire blue glance pierced through all my coverings and found me. For a moment I could feel my true self. An indescribable experience…

I came in touch with Krishna consciousness in 1984 as a teenager on the street of a small town in Poland where I grew up.

Srila Prabhupada reached me there with his Bhagavad Gita as a self made, almost unreadable copy, stitched together with a rough thread. There, for the very first time, I saw the picture of Radha Krsna and smelled beguiling fragrance of incense.

Then began my spiritual journey, the pinnacle of which was this moment when I came face to face with You.

Your smile, Your words… so gentle, charming and determinedly You snatched my heart. I used to think that was the very moment You came into my life. In time I understood that You were there before. That You have always been there. That it was Your interminable, guiding influence that even led to this meeting. The difference was that now I could slowly realize that I belong to You whereas You have always known this.

This moment was followed by ten intensive years. Yearly parikramas in the holy Dhamas and celebrating festivals with You. Hari katha spoken directly by You. Your personal darshans and the blessings to serve You. And especially and most of all Your direct instructions and Your personal letters…

It was the time where we collected the most valuable moments of our existence. Tons of them. Those invaluable dust particles that You poured so generously over each and every one of us.

Each of these precious particles charged with the potential to expand into colossal transcendental treasures. As soon as we are ready for them.

Could I at that time duly appreciate this incredible opportunity? Am I today at least somewhat capable of understanding this immensely good fortune?

Lost in this wretched, material realm, helplessly trapped by my own weaknesses and shortcomings I have been shaken by painful blows of fate. In the midst of all of this You appeared and reached out to me.

Dear Srila Gurudeva, how much gratitude can I offer You? With what sincerity? With which appropriate glorifications? None at all. Still You accept me as Your own.

You said at a darshan in Verbania, that one can always write to you, if not by post or mail, we should send it by heart. You will always answer. Yes, this is so true. And You didn't just answer. You did effect like a tsunami. You were there. Always.

The last years have been hard for us. The circumstances turned and I felt like the little bird whose beloved nest had been taken away by the ocean. I fought for it. I tried to drink the ocean drop by drop but in vain. At one point it was clear, it is over. Destroyed.

I felt like Draupadi who cannot withhold the attacks any longer, who realizes that she is doomed and that the only thing left for her to do is to raise her hands…

And at that moment, You appeared, dear Srila Gurudeva. You personally came into our home. You came and in a moment You healed all wounds. You came along with a letter that reads: "It has been Srila Gurudev'as long desire to come and stay with you and accept your worship (…)”

Now, You Srila Gurudeva, live in our small apartment with us. Fully manifested, so close and familiar as an intimate member of our small family. With my husband and my son beside me, I stand in front of You in your sapphire blue glance.

We are still astonished by our good fortune. For weeks, every time when I wake up, I think this must have been a wonderful dream. Then I get up and realize it is reality.

You are here. Your powerful presence is so evident, that we cannot imagine any more that there was a time that You have not been here, with us. And indeed, every day You teach us that there never was a time without You.

Just as always, we can talk to You, pray to You and beg You to accept our service. The only difference is, that although we are neither capable nor qualified, due to your unlimited mercy and kindness, we now have the opportunity to daily worship You, cook for You, wrap You in a warm chaddar, serve You warm drinks in between and bring You out into the midday sun.

With every step You teach us the meaning of vishrambena guru-seva.

It is 2020 and the world outside is breaking apart. You create a parallel universe in which we feel protected, sheltered and untouched by those rumbling events out there.

Kaliyuga strikes. Lockdown. Assembly ban. Travel ban. Kartik comes but India’s borders are closed. A difficult time for many…

You set out on the road. Personally, You go to Your disciples, from house to house. We may accompany You on this unforgettable Kartik-tour through the whole Germany. In the end, it became almost 2500 kilometres and more than two dozen beautiful programs.

It is so touching to experience closely how You shower the devotees with love and hope wherever You arrive. How powerful Your effect is, how You harmonize, inspire and give bliss to all. How You strengthen our feeling of belonging together and nourish the faith of each and every one.

We all felt moved back into the years 2005, 2007 or 2009. It is the same, distinctive atmosphere of Your personal presence.

It is so, because You are alive through Your wonderful disciples. They all carry You in their heart, they emanate Your shakti. Through Your disciples You are eternally present. They give class and we are listening to You. They narrate their experiences and we feel You and Your power. We celebrate Your legacy together… weep together…

We miss You bitterly…

Beloved Srila Gurudeva, You are there. Every day. Every moment. Every breath I take, You are there.

Please let me never forget it. Please let me be ever closer to You and able to realize the meaning of our eternal connection. Please make me qualified and worthy of the loving relationship with You. Guide me. Shape me. Let me grow so that one day I may serve you appropriately in Your eternal spiritual service.

Yours forever,

Manju Dasi
