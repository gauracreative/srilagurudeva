Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Harernamananda das and Shilpakarini dasi

Germany, Bremerhaven

We offer dandavat pranamas unto our diksa-guru om visnupada Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, who is very dear to Krsna, having taken shelter at His lotus feet.

Our respectful obeisances unto you, O servant of Sarasvati Gosvāmī Thakura. You are kindly preaching the message of Gaurasundara and delivering the Western countries which are filled with impersonalism and voidism.

We offer dandavat pranamas unto our siksa-guru om visnupada Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja, who is very dear to Srimati Radhika.

Srila Narayana Gosvami Maharaja is expert in describing krsna-lila. He is endowed with the qualities of magnanimity and sweetness, and he is the best of the great souls. Because he is always relishing Krsna`s sweetness, he is able to freely distribute that sweetness to others. We bow down and place our heads at his lotus feet.

Tridandi-sannyasi Srila Narayana Gosvami Maharaja, the crown-jewel of devotees, always keeps in his heart the lotus feet of Radha and Krsna, especially when Krsna serves Srimati Radhika. He deeply meditates on Sri Caitanya Mahaprabhu and the internal reasons for His descent. We bow down to the lotus feet of Srila Narayana Gosvami Maharaja, who possesses innumerable transcendental qualities.

All the experiences that we have had personally with you always inspire affection, love, commitment, deep attraction, awe and wonder.

The various festivals in the west and the parikramas in the holy dhamas were the highlights of those years, receiving many personal lessons and divine gifts from you.

Often your communications had messages on various levels, sometimes it took a while to realize their deeper meanings.

After the festivals in Germany, when you were on your way to the airport, we observed that even the rooms and hallways of your accommodation at the festival site lost that special brilliance, lustre and spiritual sparkle from your divine presence and returned to mundane grey by the hour.

Srila Gurudeva: ''What is growing in your garden?''

At another time when you kindly conducted the ceremony for offering the ashes of my deceased mother and Vrndavan das's father into the Yamuna, instead of a sombre funeral type mood, you were almost jolly. When we observed both leaf-cups with flowers and burning ghee-wicks floating down the Yamuna, you jokingly asked: ''Who will get to Kesi-ghata first?''

Srila Gurudeva, you yourself have once revealed your own identity on a morning walk in Italy at the shore of Lago Maggiore. As told by a godbrother, you were walking along the shore of the lake on your own doing some exercises. When you returned to the group accompanying you that morning, you said: ''You may think that I am just an old man walking on the shore of this lake, but in reality I am a young maiden, going with two golden pots to the Yamuna to fetch water for washing the hair of my Svamini Srimati Radhika."

On another occasion on a morning walk in Badger the theme being discussed was the golden age within Kali-yuga. When you were asked ''What is the real meaning of Golden Age?'' You stopped, looked at us and spoke two words: ''Radha-dasyam'', after which you turned around and continued walking. Your answer made that kind of stunning sense, where the realizations seem to fall like domino stones with such fresh simplicity and clarity.

You explained this point repeatedly during Kartik at Govardhana, to clarify the very goal of our existence – nothing less than “manjari-bhava - service to Srimati Radhika as Her confidential maidservants” as quoted in Caitanya Caritamrta Adi-Lila 1.4:

“May the Supreme Lord, who is known as the son of Srimati Saci-devi, be brilliantly situated in the innermost core of your hearts. Resplendent with the radiance of molten gold, He has appeared in the Age of Kali by His causeless mercy to bestow what no incarnation has ever offered before: the most sublime and radiant mellow of devotional service, manjari-bhava – service to Srimati Radhika as her confidential maidservants.”

Regardless of how far we may be removed from such a lofty goal, nothing else will ever do, all other concepts and goals have been brushed away with your explanations of angelic truths.

It was always such a great pleasure and supremely interesting to see the master of masters at work in the multifarious encounters you had with people and situations. It was breathtakingly wonderful and astonishing how much treasure of learning and enlightenment was made available by your Divine Grace, most magnanimous and glorious, always reciprocating with the thirsty yearning of our hearts. Everything done with ease of perfection at your command.

Another great quality of yours is your magnanimous forgiving response to even those who were not favourable towards you. You were transcendentally aloof and always forgiving. This is a true sign of greatness, also your attempt in building bridges to other Vaisnava groups and respecting them, looking upon all as members of Mahaprabhu's family. And astounding how your many publications impressed everyone, even those who did not like to acknowledge it, they were deeply impressed as well by your charming sweet style and superlative realizations. You perfectly grasped the true depths of our previous acaryas' histories and writings – a living example of "Guru is One''.

Regarding our present situation in this world, we believe, that the combined efforts of our Srila Prabhupada and your Divine Grace traveling the planet dozens of times, unlimited harikatha, gem-like books, and prema-atoms everywhere, is literally going to save the world right now from kali-yuga`s mad demonic calamity.

We will never forget all the realizations and spiritual insights you have bestowed upon us – we are forever indebted. We still sense your personal presence in our lives today – always accompanying us in our humble endeavours to serve and please you.

All glories, all glories to your Divine Grace – our beloved Srila Gurudeva!

Your insignificant servants

Shilpakarini dasi and Harernamananda das

(Diksa by Srila Prabhupada, Siksa by Srila Gurudeva)
