Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Aristanasana Das

France, La Nouvelle Mayapur

sri guru vandana kori tanhara carana dhari
sri bhaktivedanta prabhupada
ara yata siksa guru sabe vanca kalpataru
kripa koro ghucaka visada

I offer my humble obeisances to my spiritual master Srila A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, holding his lotus feet tightly to my heart. I also offer my humble obeisances to all my siksa gurus. They are just like wish-fulfilling trees.

I pray that they will sprinkle their mercy unto me, thereby bringing relief from my sorrows, and especially on this day of the hundredth anniversary of his appearance day, to my dearmost friend and siksa-guru parama-pujyapada Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja, I offer millions of unlimited obeisances.   

Srila Prabhupada has said in his purports, like in SB 10-12-42 "It is the duty of
the guru to explain these confidential matters to his disciple."

Srila Prabhupada has revealed all confidential matters for his many generations of disciples and grand-disciples in both diksa and siksa lines to come, in his books. He expressed himself that he wanted to develop all those confidential matters fully. Srila Prabhupada did so particularly through Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja.

Srila Prabhupada specially requested Srila Narayana Maharaja to reveal all those confidential secrets of bhakti to his disciples. And Srila Maharaja as a dearmost friend to all, 'Bhakta-bandhava', revealed in full the most excellent confidential matters of pure bhakti, Vraja bhakti and more over in Vraja bhakti, manjari-seva, the confidential service of the maidservants of Sri  Vrisabhanunandini Srimati Radhika, in the most careful manner.

If Srila Prabhupada had not written a letter in 1967 to ‘’Dear Children at New York ‘’ dated 26th July 1967 from Vrndavana, in which Srila Prabhupada wrote: ‘’ Some friends were there (at the airport) to meet us, so after clearing customs we drove into town, where we stayed at a Radha-Krishna Temple near Kashmiri Gate ’’, and a telegram, few days before, dated 19th July 1967 from New York, identifying those friends: ‘’My dear Narayana Maharaja, I am leaving New York via Air India, arriving in New Delhi on Monday 24th at 7.30 am. If you should receive this note, it would be nice if you could arrange to receive me there. I look forward to see you soon. Affectionately yours A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami."

And if Srila Prabhupada had not introduced to his ISKCON movement Srila Narayana Maharaja, by calling Srila Narayana Maharaja to his bed-side, just a few days before leaving this world, asking him to sit close to him and taking the two hands of Srila Maharaja in his own hands asking him to take care of his disciples and to give him samadhi with Srila Maharaja's own hands, how would we  have known that Srila Prabhupada had such a dear friend who would become the dear friend of all the world by revealing the deep internal moods of our Srila Prabhupada?

If Srila Narayana Maharaja had not appeared in this world, how we would have known the great wealth that our Guru Maharaja was distributing through his ISKCON movement all over the world. How we would have realized the many precious jewels Srila Prabhupada had left in this books for so many generations of devotees. Jewels like his Krishna book vol 2 ch 11: ‘’ Srila Visvanatha Cakravarti Thakura has given us a transcendental literature entitled ‘’ Krishna Bhavanamrita ‘’, which is full with Krishna's pastimes. Devotees can remain absorbed in Krishna thought by reading such books ‘’. This book ‘’Krishna Bhavanamrita ‘’ describes exclusively the service that the servants-the manjaris- of Sri Radha perform throughout the twenty four hours of a day in Radha and Krishna aprakata-lila in Vraja.

If Srila Narayana Maharaja had not come, how we would have understood the grave moods of our Guru Maharaja, expressed in his words, like in Vrndavana on 2nd August 1974: ‘’Have you found anyone who is greater than or equal to Krsna? No, yet, we can find one greater than Krsna. Radharani is greater than Krsna’’, and spread all over his books like in SB 3-15-42: ‘’ Actually the Lord’s beauty is super excellent, surpassing even the beauty of Laksmi in Vaikuntha. The devotees of the Lord in Vaikuntha want to see the Lord as the most beautiful, but the devotees in Gokula or Krishna-loka want to see Radharani as more beautiful than Krishna.’’ How we would have understood that these feelings are the feelings of those who are hundred per cent Radha-paksa, the manjaris, who are always taking the side of Sri Radha, headed by Rupa Manjari.

If Srila Narayana Maharaja had not revealed the meaning of the deep ecstasies of Srila Prabhupada like in Caitanya Caritamrita Madhya Lila- 19-210: ’’Krishna is therefore addressed by the gopis as kaitava, a great cheater, because he first attracted them to come dance with Him and yet when they actually came, neglecting the orders of their friends and relatives, He tried to avoid them by giving them so-called good instructions. These cunning instructions were too much for the gopis to tolerate; they therefore had a right to address Krsna as kaitava, a great cheater ‘’, explaining that those feelings are the profound emotions of the closest maidservants of Sri Radha, themanjaris. How we would have understood that our Srila Prabhupada was speaking as one of those nearest and dearest kinkaris of Srimati Radhika, who cannot tolerate that their Swamini is being mistreated by the dark-complexioned son of Nanda Baba, if Srila Maharaja had not explained all this?

If Srila Narayana Maharaja had not come, how  would we have established the super-excellent and immovable position of our Srila Prabhupada and his ISKCON? How high and how deep is the mission of Srila Prabhupada -  like Mount Sumeru, distributing the highest conception of Sri Rupa-Raghunatha that the service of Sri Radha is supreme, like in SB 9-24-65: ‘’The beauty of Krishna’s face was therefore much more appreciated by the gopis than by His friends the gopas or even by Yasoda Mata, who was also interested in decorating the face of Krishna ‘’, even among the Vrajavasis.

If Srila Narayana Maharaja had not appeared among us, how  would we have understood the astonishing glories of our Srila Prabhupada, that Krishna was bound to assist him in his service to Sri Radha. Prayer on board the Jaladuta:

"krishna tava punya habe bhai
sei punya karibe habe radharani kushi habe
dhruva ati boli toma tai ‘’.

O brother ! Krishna will surely bestow virtue upon you
but He will do so only when Srimati Radharani first becomes pleased.
This I declare it firmly unto you.

If Srila Narayana Maharaja had not explained all the profound ecstasies of Srila Prabhupada, hidden behind a wall of ‘’avahitta bhava’’ (the ecstasy of dissimulation) like in that same prayer written on board the Jaladuta upon arriving in America.

tomara milane bhai abara se sukha pai
gocarane ghuri dina bhora
kata vane chutachuti vane khai lutaputi
Sei dina kabe habe mora?

O my dear brother! In Your company I will experience great joy once again. Wandering about the pastures and fields I will pass the entire day with you in tending the cows joking with you and frolicking throughout the many forest of Vraja I will roll upon the ground in ecstasy when, oh when will that day be mine?

He revealed for all to relish, in many places in his transcendental books like CC 2-13-150: ‘’ It appears that Radharani is the Queen of Vrindavan and that Krishna is simply Her decoration’’;

How we would have understood that Srila Prabhupada is so much near and dear to Sri Radha and sees that Krishna is simply Her decoration as only the manjaris can see such a scene. Without Srila Maharaja, how would we have been able to understand that our Srila Prabhupada is distributing all over the world that very confidential mood of those intimate maidservants of Radharani?

Certainly Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja is the most excellent siksa disciple and dearmost friend of our Srila Prabhupada and through him Srila Prabhupada is revealing to all devotees the most confidential matters of pure bhakti as presented by Rupa-Raghunatha. The translations and tikas of Srila Narayana Maharaja on the books of Sri Rupa, Sri Raghunatha, Sri Sanatana, Sri Jayadeva, Sri Visvanatha, Sri Bhaktivinoda are the wealth of the Gaudiyas, giving great joy to the vaisnavas. Those books are the natural commentaries of the wonderful books of Srila Prabhupada like Srimad-Bhagavatam and Caitanya Caritamrita.

Who, in this world, is not drinking the nectar of those publications ?

Our ISKCON and Saraswata Gaudiya Sanga are eternally indebted toparama-pujyapada Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja for his astonishing contribution to the mission of Sri Sacinandana Gaurahari.


Aspiring for the service of Hari, Guru and Vaisnavas
Aristanasana dasa
ISKCON-New Mayapura-France
