Keshav Das

India, Faridabad

A PORTRAIT OF SRILA GURUDEVA 

YOU ARE THE ONLY GIVER!

Hare Krsna Srila Gurudeva,

Koti Koti Dandavat Pranama!

Do you remember me, my master? Even before I was born, You interacted with me. I was in the womb of my mother when she got initiation in Gopal Mantra from you. At that very moment, you blessed me. There is no doubt that whatever good I have is only given by you.

Srila Gurudeva! the second meeting was the life-changing encounter for me when you came to Faridabad and one close disciple urged you to come to our house. That time we had a small house that too on the first floor. You could not climb stairs, so your disciples carried you. During this second meeting, I was just 3-4 months old. My mother kept me on your lap, Srila Gurudeva. You asked my parents, "What is his name?". They replied, "Bharatendu". You closed your eyes for a moment and spoke, "His name is Keshav Das". You yourself changed and gave me this name "Keshav", and this is the greatest wealth I have. Do you remember all this? I don't. My mother told me about all these encounters with you. And I feel blessed whenever I recall all these encounters.

Later, during my childhood, we used to visit you every year on Guru Purnima. There, thousands of people used to come from all across the world, just to have one little glance of you, Srila Gurudeva. That time, I was too small to understand the reason for tears coming in the eyes of people there while simply seeing you Gurudeva. But now I am understanding your divine personality.

I was thirteen years old when you entered Nitya-Lila. I was unaware of your role in my life back then. I could never develop any feelings or closeness with you until I enrolled for Audio-Seva two years back in 2018. The statement that 'we can have real darsana through our ears, not through our eyes' became evident to me when every day I started hearing the sweet yet strong words of you, Srila Gurudeva. I started developing a little relationship with you. You started coming in my dreams, blessing me. I felt I was personally associating with you. Nothing was lacking. Your voice is enough to provide one with the topmost association anytime and anywhere in this world.

Unfortunately, I could never understand your glories and things that you have given me Gurudeva. But still, you made me the most fortunate, by giving me your little, yet life-changing, association.

Like the Sun, who never deprives the Earth of his sunlight, in the same way, Srila Gurudeva, you also never keep us bereaved of your blessings. I make innumerable offenses. Please rectify me. I am constantly begging for your mercy. I wrote some broken lines for you.

I was never qualified to get your mercy,
Still, u rectified me with all the courtesy

I was never inclined to accept God,
Still, you outlined the philosophy to this clod

I can never repay you till the end of the time,
But can pray to serve you with all my might

I SANG A FEW LINES FOR YOU!

May I serve you life after life.

Your grandchild,
Keshav Das
(Disciple of Sripad B.V. Siddhanti Maharaja)
