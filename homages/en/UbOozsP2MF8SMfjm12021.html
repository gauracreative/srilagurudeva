Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Manish Kashyap

India, Gurugram

Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayan Goswami Maharaja

Sri Sri Guru Gaurangau Jayatah

Shri Guru Devaya Namah

I, Manish Kashyap, son of Late B.L.Kashyap (Vinod Bihari Das) offer my dandvat pranaam in the lotus feet of our beloved Gurudeva Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja.

On the glorification of our Gurudeva, I don’t consider myself qualified enough to say anything about him, rather I feel myself privileged that he accepted, even a person like me, with his open heart. This is our Gurudeva’s quality that anyone who came to him with whatever intentions and mind set, he accepcted eveyone with open arms.

My father Sri Vinod Bihari Das (B.L. Kashyap), who was not only my first life guru but was also my siksha guru. He imbibed into me and our whole family the seed of bhakti. He only took me to our Gurudeva and got us initiated from him, without any eligibility. I still remember that it was in the year 1992 on the day of Guru Purnima when my father wanted us to take initiation. But I was denying, telling him that I am not prepared yet to take the initiation. Still he somehow made us sit in the room next to our Gurudeva’s room in Mathura Math and we directly got the Gopal mantra from Srila Gurudeva.

We didn’t realise it at that moment what it actually meant but later on got to know that it was our diksha samaroh (initiation). I am so lucky that on the very first step Gurudeva gave me Gopal Mantra, where he used to give only harinaam at first to the devotees and later on the other diksha mantras. It was like, I was straight away made to sit in class 1, skipping kinder garten and nursery classes. I give credit of all this to my father, who found us such a bonafide Gurudeva, whose only mission was to bring every living entity and mankind into bhakti, just like Sri Gauranga Mahaprabhu and Nityananda Prabhu. Otherwise, I don’t know how would I have got such an exalted Gurudeva.

Gurudeva used to visit our house and other devotee houses in Delhi and during his stay in Delhi, every day was like a grand celebration. In his presence, we used to enjoy kirtan so much, in particular his harikathas and lectures as well. We were so dumb at that time that we used to see our Gurudeva just like another Vaishnava and didn’t realise what our Gurudeva actually wanted to give us and that is why couldn’t take much of his sanga.

I remember our Gurudeva used to say with strong authority to us shishyas that no matter how much we fall down and no matter how many lives it would take, but one day he will get all of us to the lotus feet of Srimati Radha Rani and Thakur Govinda Devaji.

Now after his departure, I have certainly realised that our Gurudeva was not an ordinary Vaishnava and he is so powerful that he will definitely drag us to the lotus feet of Sri Sri Radha Shyam Sunder ji.

I pray at the lotus feet of Srila Gurudeva to keep his mercy on me and my whole family. May we all never forget him, his teachings, his bhajans & kirtans and may his divine legacy continue till eternity.

Aspiring for the mercy of Srila Gurudeva,

Manish Kashyap
