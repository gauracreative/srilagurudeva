Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Bihari dasa

Germany, Berlin

om ajnana-timirandasya jnananjana-salakaya
caksur unmilitam yema tasmai sri gurave namah

nama om visnu-padaya radhikayai priyatmane
sri-srimad-bhaktivedanta-narayana iti namine

vande ham sri-guru-varam sri-rupanuga-pravaram
vraja-rasa-rasikam ca narayanam tam prapannam

sri-guru-caranam-vande ramana-presthaya-bhutale
rupanuga-bhakti-dam ca krpa murti narayanam

sri-krsna-lila-kathane su-daksam
audariya-madhuriya-gunais ca yuktam
varam varenyam purusam mahantam
narayanam tvam sirasa namami

I offer my humble obeisances at the lotus feet of nitya-lila pravista om visnupada astottara-sata Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja.

Srila Gurudeva's sublimity and magnitude are infinite. After Srila Swami Prabhupada, no one explained and established Gaura-tattva (like the three reasons why Sri Caitanya Mahaprabhu descended in this material world, and his innermost moods of Srimati Radharani and transcendental emotions of immersing in Sri Krsna) like Srila Gurudeva. Or what Srimati Radharani thinks, when she sees Krsna, and the happiness and ecstasy she experiences....

Nobody established those topics with such ease and strong philosophy thru their lectures and books (which he wrote mainly in the last thirty years of his life) like Srila Gurudeva did. After Srila Swami Prabhupada left our vision, he made sure his disciples do not loose direction.

We can't really understand what Srila Gurudeva bequeath to this world. But we can say, that he was like Srila Prabhupada, in terms of explaining every topic all patiently and in a way that was easily understandable by us. This does not minimize Srila Prabhupada's magnitude at all, I only dare to say, that Srila Gurudeva was a great personality like Srila Prabhupada, and that he is not less in realization than Srila Prabhupada was. The books Srila Gurudeva manifested will shine like many Suns and will enlighten our future for generations to come.

Srila Gurudeva was such a divine personality, that he would know all of our thoughts, feelings and anarthas as if he can read our minds. He established the importance of being completely focused and the infinite nectar of Sri Sri Radha-Krsna's pastimes would always flow from his lotus lips like a constant stream.

I mercifully got the opportunity to listen to this divine nectar in the form of Harikatha for twenty years from his lotus mouth, and I have never heard such sweetness and variety from anyone else before that.

Before I met Srila Gurudeva for the first time, I wrote him a letter asking a question and he wrote back, that he is happy that I am doing nama sankirtan in the streets, but that I should know that more important than that is the association with high class sadhus (sadhu-sanga). He never ever said something like: "I don´t know about this topic." As a completely realized maha bhagavata, he was able to answer any question, he knew everything. He knows the question in ones heart, even before one would speak it out. Like Sri Krsna knows past, present and future, his pure devotee is omniscient too. Gurudeva once revealed to us, that he could see our past 100 lives as well as 100 future lifetimes only by looking at our forehead.

He always spoke the irrevocable truth... such as that we are coming from tatastha sakti... that we have never been in Goloka Vrndavan... and much more tattva. He is the Protector of the truth, as-it-is.

At the festivals with Srila Gurudeva, I felt, that everyone was in bliss and that only through Srila Gurudeva's presence the worries of thousands of people would just disappear. He would transform every place where he resided into Vrindavan. I remember, that in those transcendental festivals we were all in great ecstasy and the sweetness of Srila Gurudeva's Harikatha left us immersed in this ocean of happiness.

Jay Srila Gurudeva!

Your fallen servant,

Bihari Das
