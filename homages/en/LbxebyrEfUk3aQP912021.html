Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Taruni Gopi Dasi

Netherlands, Zoetermeer

om ajnana timirandasya jnananjana salakaya
caksur unmilitam yena tasmai sri gurave namah

nama om visnupadaya gaura presthaya bhutale
sri srimad bhaktivedanta vana itinamine

nama om visnupadaya radhikayai priyatmane
sri srimad bhaktivedanta narayan itinamine

sri krsnalila kathane sudaksam audarya madhurya gunaistya yuktam
varam varenyam purusam mahantam narayanam tvam sirasa namami

gurave gouracandraya radhikayai tadalaye
krishnaya krishnabhaktaya tadbhaktaya namo namah

vancha kalpa tarurabhaysca krpasindhubhya eva ca
patitanam pavanebhyo vaisnavebhyo namo namah

sri krsna caitanya prabhu nityananda sri advaita
gadadhar srivasadi sri gaura bhakta vrnda

hare krsna hare krsna krsna krsna hare hare
hare rama hare rama rama rama hare hare

First of all, I would like to offer my humble obesiances unto the lotus feet of my beloved diksha Gurudeva Om Visnupada astottara satta Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Vana Gosvami Maharaj. Secondly, I offer my unlimited respectful obesiances unto the lotus feet of my beloved siksha Gurudeva Om Visnupad astotara satta Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Siddhanti Gosvami Maharaj. Thirdly, I offer my respectful dandavat pranams unto the divine, supremely merciful lotus feet of my Paramgurudeva Nitya lila pravista Om Visnupad astotara satta Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja, to whom I would like to offer this puspañjali. Being aware of the fact that I am always engrossed in worldly pleasures and have no qualification to offer my Paramgurudeva anything, I humbly request him to be merciful on me and accept this offering.

(I will now further refer to Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayan Gosvami Maharaj as ‘Paramgurudeva’.)

I didn’t have the fortune of meeting Paramgurudeva while he was physically present on this earth, but somehow the other, out of his unlimited compassion, I have heard some of Paramgurudeva’s glories from his dear disciples: my dikshagurudeva and sikshagurudevas.

I heard that Paramgurudeva appeared in Bihar, and I became very happy when I heard that, because my great-grandparents are also from Bihar. I immediately felt a connection with him.

I heard that Paramgurudeva was one of the pillars of the Gaudiya Vedanta Samiti, together with his godbrothers Nitya lila pravista Om Visnupad astotara satta Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Vamana Gosvami Maharaj and Nitya lila pravista om visnupad astotara satta Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Trivikram Gosvami Maharaj.

I also heard that when Paramgurudeva’s book came out, he was as excited as a child. This shows how much importance he gave to the publishing of books.

I heard that Paramgurudeva was also very athletic. He used to run 8 kilometres to school, barefoot. In the book ‘Srila Gurudeva, the Supreme treasure’, written by Pujyapad Bhaktivedanta Madhav Maharaj, I read that Paramgurudeva worked as a police officer. One day, there was a fitness-test. All the policemen had to run to the end of the field and then run back, it was a competition. Everyone started to run, it was a very long distance. Srila Paramgurudeva was very clever, he ran the half of the distance, then ran back and was the first arriving at the start. Some of his fellow policeman started to complain, but when Paramgurudeva asked: ‘You want to do it again?’’, they all stopped complaining as they where too tired to run again.

I also heard another pastime that happened when Paramgurudeva worked at the police, was that he was so absorbed in chanting the holy names, he forgot to go to work on time. The next day, Paramgurudeva went to the office. Everyone there said Paramgurudeva was present yesterday and did much work. Paramgurudeva then understood that it was Krishna Himself who took Paramgurudeva’s form and went to office. This pastime shows what kind of high-class devotee Paramgurudeva is and what a close relationship he has with Sri Krishna.

Something that really struck me is Paramgurudeva’s kirtan. On YouTube, there is one mahamantra kirtan, and when you play that kirtan, you can just feel the separation and love for Sri Sri Radha Krishna through Paramgurudeva’s voice.

Always when I look at pictures of Paramgurudeva, he looks so beautiful and powerful. He has got beautiful blue eyes.

In January or February 2020, under the guidance of Pujyapad Bhaktivedanta Siddhanti Maharaj, there was a project called: ‘Srila Gurudeva in every house’. The padukas of Paramgurudeva were brought to houses of devotees, there the padukas were kept on an asan in the house, kirtan was done and harikatha was given. The padukas were brought to my house as well, and as it is said that the clothes of a Vaisnava are non-different from him, Paramgurudeva himself actually came in my house. That day, there was such a blissful atmosphere in the house, and everyone was dancing with the kirtan.

In the book ‘Srila Gurudeva, the Supreme Treasure’ I read about the bhajan ‘Vraja-jana-mana-sukhakari’. It was written that by singing this bhajan, the desire of serving Gurudeva in his spiritual form will arise in the heart. I also read that Paramgurudeva conquered the heart of his Gurudeva by serving the vaisnava Ananga Mohan brahmacari. This story inspires me to serve the vaisnavas. In the same book is also written that we can massage Gurudeva externally by massaging his feet, but we can also massage the heart of Gurudeva by trying to understand his mood. I felt really inspired by reading this.

Paramgurudeva wanted everyone to hear Hari-Katha, without any discrimination. Thus he has been so merciful that he inspired devotees to set up the audio-sevateam. I think this really shows how merciful he is, as many devotees who didn’t get the opportunity to serve him directly and didn’t get the opportunity to hear harikatha from him directly, can now, by doing audio-seva, still serve Paramgurudeva and hear his harikatha. This audio-seva is not only beneficial for those who do the seva, but for the whole world. One of the quotes of Paramgurudeva is: ‘the world will be amazed by what I have given to them’. I am sure that when someone, with a sincere heart, tries to serve Paramgurudeva in any way, he will certainly acknowledge it!

So in conclusion, Paramgurudeva is a supremely merciful and glorious acarya. There are so many glories of Paramgurudeva, it is impossible to describe them all, but I hope he is pleased with my attempt to speak some of his glories. Everything which happened in my life on the path of bhakti, I consider solemnly as the mercy of my Paramgurudeva and his associates. It is my earnest desire that one day in my life I will be able to serve him with a 100 % surrender mood, under the guidance of my Gurudeva. May he bestow his blessings upon me so I can one day become a bona fide disciple. Please forgive me in case I’ve said something wrong.

Vancha kalpa tarurabhaysca krpasindhubhya eva ca patitanam pavanebhyo vaisnavebhyo namo namah

Nitya lila pravista om visnupad astotara satta Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja ki JAYA!

Ananta koti vaisnava vrnda ki jaya!

Nitai Gaura Premanande Hari Haribol!!

Your eternal servant,

TaruniGopi Dasi
Holland

(Disciple of Sri Sripad Bhaktivedanta Vana Goswami Maharaj
