Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Sadananda Dasa

France, Bordeaux

oṁ ajñāna-timirāndhasya
jñānāñjana-śalākayā
cakṣur unmīlitaṁ yena
tasmai śrī-gurave namaḥ

I was born in the darkest ignorance, and my spiritual master opened my eyes with the torch of knowledge. I offer my respectful obeisances unto him.

śrī-kṛṣṇa-līlā-kathane su-dakṣam
audārya-mādhurya-gunaiś ca yuktam
varaṁ varenyaṁ puruṣaṁ mahāntaṁ
nārāyanaṁ tvām śirasā namāmi

Srila Narayana Maharaja is an expert in describing krsna-lila. He is endowed with the qualities of magnanimity and sweetness, and he is the best of the great souls. Because he is always relishing Krsna’s sweetness, he is able to freely distribute that sweetness to others. I bow down and place my head at his lotus feet.

bhayaṁ dvitīyābhiniveśataḥ syād
īśād apetasya viparyayo ’smṛtiḥ
tan-māyayāto budha ābhajet taṁ
bhaktyaikayeśaṁ guru-devatātmā
–Srimad Bhagavatam (11.2.37)

Fear arises when a living entity misidentifies himself as the material body because of absorption in the external, illusory energy of the Lord. When the living entity thus turns away from the Supreme Lord, he also forgets his own constitutional position as a servant of the Lord. This bewildering, fearful condition is effected by the potency for illusion, called māyā. Therefore, an intelligent person should engage unflinchingly in the unalloyed devotional service of the Lord, under the guidance of a bona fide spiritual master, whom he should accept as his worshipable deity and as his very life and soul.

kṛṣṇa bhuli' sei jīva anādi-bahirmukha
ataeva māyā tāre deya saṁsāra-duḥkha
–Caitanya Caritamrta (Madhya 20.117)

Forgetting Krsna, the living entity has been attracted by the external feature from time immemorial. Therefore the illusory energy (maya) gives him all kinds of misery in his material existence.

How can we live or even survive in this material world, where we see a lot of suffering, pride, greed, ignorance, false ego, illusion where everyone is caught in the wheel of endless samsara. How much effort it takes to get some material pleasure that is limited in time. No one can escape his destiny in this material universe, to collect the fruits of his actions whether good or bad; never without unconditional mercy, without the presence of a pure devotee of the Lord, can one achieve liberation.

But what is liberation? The pure devotee of the Lord will give us real liberation, the love of God through devotion.

The uttama-Vaisnava guru plants the seed of pure bhakti and like a kind gardener he will uproot the bad plants with our consent or even by force and turn all things of bad omens into nectar. He gives us the favourable tools through our sadhana, through his instructions, his glance, his presence, his prasadam, his singing, he gives us the intelligence required for our evolution, he changes our material karma to orient it in the path of pure bhakti; he helps us to cut the knots of our bodily attachments, that we have carefully consolidated since time immemorial, with the sword of spiritual knowledge; it makes us strong inwardly, transforms our obstacles into unexpected and exceptional tests of life, it pushes our limits, helps us to get out of our material comfort and also in our practice mixed with pleasure of the senses, to put us focus on the exclusive, immaculate bhakti.

Srila Gurudeva is always present, closer to us than our vital air; it is up to us to perceive him, to listen to him, to recognize the signs of his limitless goodness because he is the embodiment of unlimited mercy, unconditional love, the blissful energy of Krsna, Srimati Radharani, in the form of a manjari.

How can one conceive that Gurudeva is both as innocent as a little child and omniscient?

Obviously since he has the same transcendental characteristics as Krsna. Those who have approached Srila Gurudeva closely or even other pure Vaisnavas have had the opportunity to perceive this marvellous characteristic that subjugates us. Sri Guru is everywhere.

It is time to conceive of Srila Gurudeva as the sum total of all gurus, an incarnation of Nityananda Prabhu or Balarama Prabhu, and to recognize his absolute presence; he knows the past, present and future of each of his disciples. The first time I met Gurudeva, he scanned me completely with his pure love, I had nothing more to hide from him, impossible to escape, my duplicity was exposed. He knows me better than I know myself; He knows everything like the caitya-guru.

I know very little about the glories of the guru-parampara but I met Srila Gurudeva who just gave me a drop of this spiritual faith, he shared with me this drop coming from the ocean of prema, of pure love in which he himself constantly bathed, it saved me for the first time from my distress, which finally gave meaning to my life. Gurudeva stole my soul so that one day he will offer it at the feet of Srimati Radharani. But sometimes this can only be done through an intense physical and mental cleansing that is at the height of the goal.

My desire to serve Krsna, no matter how small, is there because of Gurudeva who has allowed me to chant the holy names, to have the company of the Vaisnavas, to come to India to participate in the parikramas, to study the holy scriptures, to visit the temples, to have the darsana of so many vigrahas, to have invited me to stay in the Gaudiya Matha, but this desire is so overwhelmed by so much lust like a mountain of ignorance. But how can one cleanse a mind that has been soiled by material desires since time immemorial?

Gurudeva once said that it is practically impossible to offend the guru because one is very careful, but occasionally one can neglect other high Vaisnavas and thus the plant of bhakti could be uprooted. Krsna will not forgive. The Guru will, but only if we repent. I hope never to offend the Vaisnava again and I pray every day to Gurudeva to never abandon me in spite of my poor devotion and begging for his mercy.

Do the tests, the sufferings I am experiencing come from my karma or have I offended the Vaisnavas? Difficult to know, but in any case, let me welcome this test as a mercy from Krsna to help me reform myself and to go deeper in my faith. Gurudeva surely wants to lead me a life of total surrender and therefore to serve him in a feeling of 'Do or Die', to transform life's trials into a test of faith, of devotion.

Once Gurudeva said that he had the right to operate on us being a 'spiritual surgeon'. I am currently living with a disease that has been chasing me for a long time but I see clearly how my karma is being changed by the grace of Srila Gurudeva who protects me and makes me understand that only he and the Vaisnavas can save me from drowning in the whirlpool of material existence.

Gurudeva has given me the opportunity to serve him in this challenge, a turning point in my life, through a real awareness due to this symbolic death, my handicap, which is ultimately only an illusion, but which in my conditioned state has made me realize that this earthly world is not our true home. The initiatory illness becomes a source of spiritual awakening, and should make me humble.

Gurudeva gives me the opportunity to try to be a little bit sincere by involving myself concretely, even far from the holy places in services such as the translation of his books, my bhajana-band, connection with my spiritual family and now with the Audio-Seva team, in the listening and restoration of the 12000 lectures that will change and greatly influence the whole world. Working with the restoration of Gurudeva's lectures also allowed me to rediscover in an intense way how his harikatha is absolutely incredible and unimaginable for a conditioned soul.

This is a miracle in my situation because I am no longer working. All I have to do is to serve Gurudeva, and this service is completely within my capabilities. I obviously have less and less contact with the mundane world. Illness becomes a blessing and somehow gives me no more choice but to be determined in my practice and finally hold on to the rope of mercy and never let go.

I‘m speaking of my personal story only in order to glorify Srila Gurudeva. For I am not even the worm in excrement, I am the excrement filled with lust incompatible with the purity of unconditional love. But this condition is only temporary, it has no connection with my spiritual soul eternally bound to Krsna that Gurudeva will reveal to me. It is not blind faith; this faith is connected with concrete experiences of the spiritual life of initiation with a well-established process that has already proven itself since the beginning of time. I do not know of any other spirituality that is so complete, there is no such thing. Bhakti yoga, the devotional love to Sri Radha Krsna, to Gauranga Mahaprabhu gives everything. It is a reservoir of knowledge, eternity and bliss like no other and Srila Gurudeva is one of the main ambassadors.

All glories to Srila Gurudeva!

All glories to Vaisnavas!

Gaura Nitai!

Radhe Syama!

Sadananda Dasa
