Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Asha Lata Dasi

United States, New York

All glories to Sri Sri Hari, Guru and Vaisnavas. My heartfelt pushpanjali in the following words at the lotus feet of my beloved Gurudeva, nitya-lila pravista om visnupada astottara-sata Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Goswami Maharaja.

How I met my beloved Gurudeva is a long story and how he made my life meaningful is impossible to describe in a few lines. I do not have eligibility to glorify my eternal spiritual master with this material tongue, still I would like to share some treasured moments on the occasion of our beloved Gurudeva’s centennial.

I was born in a religious Tiwari family in Northeastern India, Bihar, from where Gurudeva started his lila.

In childhood we were only taught to take care of our gross body, to eat well, to study well, to hold materialistic ambitions and achieve material success to have better life and a better livelihood. With some sukriti I was fortunate enough to be married into a Krishna conscious family but it was difficult for me to understand to do just harinama, instead of doing your own karma especially when you are a physician. I always thought that serving a human being is equal to serving the Lord. I used to justify it by saying that a human being is a part and parcel of the Lord and by providing service to human beings, I am providing service to the Lord too.

This kind of struggle was happening in my mind for a long time. I was very successful in my profession and when I migrated to America I had a chance to go to the temple and meet the devotees and preachers. Still, I was not very much inclined to follow any one as my faith was shaken so many times. My heart was hankering to follow a sadguru.

By mercy of Lord Krishna, my nephew gave me a book of Srila Gurudeva to read and glorified Gurudeva whenever we talked.

I had the good fortune of hosting few house programs of senior disciples of Srila Gurudeva who ignited in my heart desire to meet Sril Gurudeva and finally in 2006 we went to Florida to have his darshan.

I cannot describe it in words, his blue lotus eyes were deep like ocean full of love and compassion for conditioned souls like me. He cleared my several doubts in a few minutes by his eternal Vani.

I prayed in my heart that he should accept me as his disciple and to my surprise he accepted me as aspiring servant of Sri Hari, Sri Guru and Vaisnavas by giving me diksha and giving me first eternal sambandha. That was in the year 2006.
Few years passed in his association in USA and India and he started nursing my soul with harikatha and providing Vaisnava association. I was in bliss and humming like bee around him and started following the process instructed by him (Sri guru nistha is the back bone of bhakti).

It's very hard to describe the attachment which I feel for the lotus feet of my spiritual master. I want to be committed and enthusiastic to propagate the mission that he came to give to the conditioned souls of this material world. He's the only one who is helping all of us to do this in this life, and beyond... till we do not get perfection.

I'm praying and have full trust that he will keep guiding me, in the same way till the end of my conditioned life.

After having a few days’ association, we came home but I left my heart there. I had a good fortune of being graced by his mercy again in Hawaii with my husband and my son.

In his close association I collected my strength to clear the doubts of my heart which I never shared with anyone before. My sraddha in the lotus feet of my Gurudeva was becoming stronger and stronger and the only desire I was having was to serve him in anyway. He graced me by accepting to visit New York and giving me opportunities to serve him with all his associates. Guru knows the heart of disciple. I never witnessed any festival before like this when Srila Gurudeva arrived at our home surrounded by a huge crowd of devotees singing harinama and glorifying his presence with kartal and mridanga. It seemed as the lord has come from spiritual world to earth.

And why not?
Guru is sakshat Hari!

The whole atmosphere was blissful. Everyone from different states and cities of USA assembled to have a glimpse of Srila Gurudeva and he showered his unconditional mercy on everyone without caring for his health and comfort.
“Jaya, jaya Gurudeva” - this sound is still ringing in my ears. It was only for a few days. But that was the time which cannot be described in words.

I met so many of my god sisters. Before meeting them I was under the impression that all the mercy of my spiritual master was showered on me and only I was fortunate to have all his love and affection but to my surprise when they narrated their stories of association with Srila Gurudeva, I felt what I got was nothing. This is called real guru. He has tremendous and inexhaustible mercy.

Once in Houston we wanted our spiritual master to speak harikatha in Hindi, which we heard is more melodious and heart touching (though there were several devotees who cannot understand Hindi that well). He started in Hindi, out of his bhakta vatsalya but then he started speaking in English again. We were disappointed but to our surprise he then started speaking Ramayana Katha in Hindi . All the devotees in the assembly were spellbound listening and feeling the mood of Srila Gurudeva. Tears were flowing from everyone’s eyes and also from the eyes of our beloved Gurudeva.

It is not the language but the mood which Sri Guru transmits in the heart of disciple. It was a new lesson and realization of bhakti. Jaya, jaya Gurudeva.
He took us in that moment to what really happened in the story and I can never never never never forget that story.

Another instance was in Orlando. I had darshan of Srila Gurudeva. He looked totally aloof to his material surrounding. To me he was in a different world, very quiet. I asked him “Srila Gurudeva I want to serve you, I can cook for you, as I did in the past”. To my surprise he replied “I do not remember anything except Krishna. If you know cooking you can do today”. I was happy as I can cook his favourite dish, dal puri. I made some puris for bhoga but decided to serve him a hot one when he is honoring prasadam so I requested his servant to remind me when it was time to serve and went to take rest. The servant, however, offered him lunch prasadam and forgot to remind me. I was awakened by a dream and without any confrontation quickly cooked one puri and surpassing all obstacles offered him and begged in my heart to accept it. Srila Gurudeva relished it and blessed me to cook like that and serve Thakurji.

During his sickness pastime in 2010 I reached Govardhan from USA. I wanted to inquire about his health and was fortunate enough to go to his room. I saw Srila Gurudeva lying in bed with several tubes attached to his body. I was heartbroken, feeling the deep pain his fingers and his legs which were swollen. After properly examining him medically, I stared massaging his fingers and legs but always looking at his eyes waiting for them to open and see what I am doing but he did a favor on me by breaking my pride and did not utter anything or open his eyes during my one hour of stay. I had to leave and I asked Vishnu prabhu to do the same during his stay there. As soon as I left he asked Vishnu prabhu if I had left. This was told to me by Vishnu prabhu. Guru knows all but only reveals what good for us. Jaya, jaya Gurudeva!

Another treasurable moment was during the last day of my stay at Govardhan when I decided to take diksha from Gurudeva. With all preparation I approached Madhav Maharaj and requested for same and sat near Srila Gurudeva’s lotus feet bowing down with the desire that he should see that I am there and asking for divya jnana and requesting him to burn my all anarthas.

Again I had pride in my heart and Gurudeva did not look at me. BV Madhava maharaja gave me diksha mantra written on paper and I had to leave after some time for parikrama without being acknowledged by Srila Gurudeva. With a heavy heart I left. As soon as I reached first halt of parikrama I got a text from one of my god sisters (with whom I used to serve gurudeva during my Govardhan stay), She was asking where I was and that Srila Gurudeva was asking why I did not come today?

Jaya, jaya Gurudeva. Always showering his mercy and love. Always purifying conditioned souls. Gurudeva’s mercy is countless and cannot be described. Praying for his mercy to make me eligible to become his pure disciple and fulfil his mission. Jaya, jaya Gurudeva!

My gross body was taken care of by my biological father who helped me become a doctor which helped me in serving my spiritual father during his sickness pastime and getting his tremendous mercy in understanding the goal of human life.

Aspiring for service of Sri Hari, Sri Guru and Vaisnavas,
Asha lata dasi
