Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Krsna Jivani Dasi

Brazil, Pirenópolis, Goiás

To my beloved Srila Gurudeva, nitya lila pravista om vishnupad 108 Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanda Narayna Goswami Maharaj, from the core of my heart my humble and sincere obeisances.

namo om visnu-padaya radhykayai priyatmane
sri-srimad-bhaktivedanta-narayana iti namine
sri-krishna-lila-kathane sudakshan audarya-madhurya-gunaish ca yuktam
varam varenyam purusham mahantam narayanam tvam shirasa namani
tridandinam bhakta-shiromanim ca sri-krsna-padabja-dhrtaika-hrdi
caitanya-lilamrta-sara-saranam narayanam tvam satatam prapadye

I personally met Srila Gurudeva at 2000 in Brasil. Six months before I had met with Sripad Bhaktivedanta Vana Maharaj and Sriman Govinda Bhakta Prabhu (Sripad Ashram Maharaj), who gave me some of Gurudeva's books. These books took my heart, they inspired me, they filled my soul with love and affection and made me sure of the path and goal of bhakti.

They insisted that I go to festival to meet with Srila Gurudeva Bhaktivedanta Narayana Goswami Maharaj, and listen to his powerful harikatha. And so, it was.

We stayed in the kirtan, waiting for Srila Gurudeva for more than two hours. While he was making this journey from the airport to the festival.

We were very anxious, seniors and juniors, to meet him. Whenever Srila Gurudeva will get out of the car, at that moment the commotion was general, his presence was so strong that it flooded the whole space with transcendental love, devotion for vaisnavas and Radha-Krsna. I cannot find words to describe this sensation.

I couldn’t see Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Maharaja. I could only see Srila A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami, our Srila Prabhupada.

I ran, pushed and screamed, hail Srila Prabhupada, hail Srila Prabhupada, until the moment when Srila Gurudeva sat on vyasasana and I managed to bow down to him. When I got out, I saw my Srila Prabhupada get inside Srila Gurudeva. Only then I saw his blue eyes.

At that moment, I was sure that my Srila Prabhupada, despite having done little service to him, has taken my hand and led me to the lotus feet of Srila Gurudeva, Bhaktivedanta Narayana Goswami Maharaj. I realized that all real gurus on the gaudiya vaisnavas, rupanugas are interwoven like the weave of a fabric. That power of the bonafide guru is unique, akhanda guru tattva.

Srila Gurudeva came to give me the true understanding of the harinama, came to give the most beautiful path and service through the service to the Vaisnavas, thru sadhu sanga. He came to give the real concept of rupanuga service to the divine coupe Sri Sri Radha Krishna, came to show Caitanya Mahaprabhu’s identity and desire.

“I was wandering in this universe according to my karma and only because of your causeless mercy I was able to have a drop of the ocean of your association. You planted in my heart the most beautiful seed, of love to the guru, vaishnavas and Radha Krsna.”

My Srila Gurudeva always was very merciful with me. Although I’am always absorbed in myself, he gave me everything what a person needs to go on the path of bhakti: seva, sadhu sanga, nama, diksa and love of Sri Sri Radha Govindaji. He lit the fire of desire for bhakti in my heart.

The last time when I was physically present with Srila Gurudeva was at his Vyasa Puja 2010, Brasil.

Srila Gurudeva was always very kind to me, every time he would address me, giving an instruction, a blessing or asking about my well being.

Srila Gurudeva gave me many blessings. For a long time I believed that he thought I was very foolish, because he was so generous with me, like we are normally with the children. One day by his own mercy, I realized that he loves me and loves all unconditionally, hence he distributed the highest blessings indiscriminately, even to people like me.

Although Sri Krishna says “I’m not different than the guru.”

“All I know is you, I don’t know Krishna’s mercy only the guru’s mercy, I only know his penetrating gaze, I only know his smile, I only know his blessings”.

Mahaprabhu asked to Sri Ramananda Ray:

“Of all types of suffering, which is the most painful?”

Sri Ramananda answered:

“Apart from the separation of the devotee of Krishna, I do not know any other unhappiness, that is more unbearable”

My Gurudeva entered nitya lila, but internally and externally he is always taking care of me, of us. He told me, "I never, never, never will give up you, and always will send Sripad Van Maharaj to help you. Srila Gurudeva is every day showing us how to develop the bhakti process and how to develop love and affection for Vaisnavas, Radha and Krishna in different ways.

Srila Gurudeva, I hope one day I will be able to completely surrender unto your lotus feet. So I pray at your lotus feet and all the Vaisnavas that my desire be fulfilled one day and thus I could develop the seed of prema bhakti that you planted in my heart.

Following the steps of acaryas and for not knowing how to express myself properly, I offer this prayer to you.

nama srestha manum api saci putram atra svarupam
rupam tasyagrajam uru purim mathurim gosthavatim
Radha kundam giri varam aho! radhika madhavasam
prapto yasya prathita krpaya sri gurum tam nato smi
–Sri Mukta carita by Srila Raghunatha Dasa Goswami

“I bow down at the beautiful lotus feet of my spiritual master, by whose causeless mercy, I have obtained the supreme holy name, the divine mantra, and the service of the son of Saci-mata, the association of Srila Svarupa Damodara, Rupa Goswami and his older brother Sanatana Goswami, the supreme abode of Mathura, the blissful abode of Vrindavana, the divine Radha-kunda, Govardhana Hill, and the desire within my heart for the loving service to Sri Radhika and Madhava in Vrndavana”

Aspiring for one particle of dust of your lotus feet and be a servant of your servant of your servant forever.

Krsna Jivani Dasi.

Brazil/2021
