Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Sripad B.V. Shanta Maharaja

United States

nama om visnu padaya radhikayah priyatmane
sri srimad bhaktivedanta narayana iti namine

om ajnana timirandhasya jnananjana salakaya
caksur unmilitam yena tasmai sri gurave namah

After so much delay and with a lot of trepidation I sit down to write an offering to try and glorify my divine spiritual preceptor nitya-lila pravista om visnupada astottara-sata Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja. It is only due to his causeless mercy that such a lowly person as myself could ever dream of such a sad-guru in my life.

As an ignorant fool from time immemorial I have forgotten my constitutional position as a servant of Krsna. I have become a servant of Maya. Thus, I have been recycled in the in the ocean of repeated birth and death. As a result, my existence from one life to another is ensnared in fear and unhappiness. There is no peace; there has never been any peace. Somehow out of the patched wilderness of my hopelessness has shone forth this saintly, beautiful, and bountifully magnanimous personality offering to help me relieve my calamitous nightmare.

brahmanda bhramite kona va bhagyavan jiva
guru krsna prasade paya bhakti lata bija
–Sri Caitanya-Caritamrta by Srila Krsnadasa Kaviraja Gosvami (Madhya-lila 19.151)

According to their karma, all living entities are wandering throughout the entire universe. By dint of good fortune, out of the trillions of these lost souls, one may have the opportunity for the mercy of both Sri Guru and Krsna who plant the seed of bhakti in his heart.

Even though I do not process any good quality, Srila Gurudeva by his causeless mercy has afforded me the opportunity to associate with his disciples. You have sowed the seed of bhakti in my heart. It is now up to me to nourish this seed of bhakti according to your directions. Bless me to always be in association of bona fide sadhus.

To those who may doubt who is a bona fide Guru please hear from the sastric evidence:

tasmad gurum prapadyeta jijnasuh sreya uttamam
sabde pare ca nisnatam brahmany upasamasrayam
–Srimad Bhagavatam (11.3.21)

A person who seriously desires to attain the ultimate spiritual perfection must seek out a bona fide Guru and take shelter of him. The qualifications Sri Guru is as follows:

A) Sabda-brahma: he has fully realized the vedic scriptural conclusions

B) Para-brahma: he knows the Supreme Absolute Truth

C) Niskincana: for whom worldly attainment and clamor has no charm

Like our Srila A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, all the qualities of the maha-bhagavatas spoken of in the sastras could readily be found in Srila Gurudeva. The simplicity and humility attracted me first and foremost to your lotus feet. Gurudeva, you wanted the highest goal for all devotees. You are kripa-sindhu, an ocean of kindness. So even in an advanced age where you could have stayed in Vrndavana absorbed in Radha Krsna Yugala bhajana, you circled the globe 32 times for sake of delivering the conditioned souls from their deplorable situations. I vividly recollect when you mercifully blessed my home when I was a householder with your divine presence. You would sit on the lawn facing the mountain. You said, when you see that mountain you were looking at Sri Giriraja Gorvadana. Srila Gurudeva, you were in reality revealing to us your transcendental nature that never leaves Vrndavana.

Gurudeva, even though you came to give us Krsna, you said, "I want you to forget that Krsna is God." That threw many devotees off balance. Yet Srila Gurudeva, in our astonished stupor we listened and watched you patiently and expertly dissect and divulge the rasa-tattva of the ragatmika and the raganuga bhaktas of Vrajabhumi. You brought alive the moods of the sakhas like Sridama and Sudama, the vatsalya-bhava of Yasodamaya and Nanda Baba, and above all the bhava of the gopis in madhurya-rasa. Gurudeva, you taught us to aim high for the service at Srimati Radharani's lotus feet in the mood of the manjaris. You taught us the inner meanings of Lord Caitanya Mahaprabhu's teachings. You mercifully gave us the transcendental books that will help us.

Gurudeva, you never got tired. With your constant travels, you always looked youthful, fresh and alert to deal with our mundane as well as our spiritual needs. You were the loving father, mother, teacher, and the grand preceptor to all. You have shown us by example how you are a direct representative of Krsna.

saksad-dharitvena samasta-sastrair
uktas tatha bhavyata eva sadbhih
kintu prabhor yah priya eva tasya
vande guroh sri-caranaravindam
–Sri Gurvastakam by Srila Visvanatha Cakravarti Thakura (Verse 7)

All the scriptures proclaim Sri Guru to be saksad-hari-tva, endowed with the potency of Sri Hari, and he is also considered by all the great saints to be His direct representative. Indeed, Sri Guru is very dear to the Lord, being His confidential servitor. I offer prayers unto his lotus feet.

Though I have no any qualification, you personally gave me so much mercy. Srila Gurudeva, your personal instructions directly and indirectly have propelled me in my quest to fight harder to reach the ultimate goal of Krsna-prema. If by any weakness I fall into the dangerous ocean of material existence, Srila Gurudeva, I beg you, have mercy upon me. Pluck me forcefully from the jaws of these ferocious marauding beasts of material desires and attachments. This is my own prayer, my lord and master.

B.V. Shanta Swami
