Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Manimati devi Dasi

Costa Rica, San Jose

Bhaktivedanta Narayan Goswami Maharaja

Ofrezco mis humildes reverencias a los pies de loto de mi querido Gurudeva, Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja.

Durante algunos años no pude tomar darshan físico de Srila Gurudeva, sin embargo, en ese período, a pesar hablar muy poco inglés, logré tener comunicación vía correo electrónico con él.

Hasta que llegó el buen día en que pude viajar a California, al templo de Venice. Tenía 4 años orando por ese momento, y por fin iba a tomar darshan de Srila Gurudeva. No dejaba de pensar cuántas vidas llevaba esperando tener la oportunidad de conocer a un vaisnava puro.

Todos entraban al templo, los devotos que me acompañaban de Costa Rica pasaron. Pero yo me desmoroné, entré al salón donde se dejaban los zapatos y no tenía la fuerza de mirar adentro.

Saber que un asociado directo de Sri Sri Radha Krsna estaba ahí tan cerca, a solo unos pasos de distancia. Esa personalidad que es Sakshad Hari, que es un océano de misericordia, el único que nos puede sacar del ciclo de nacimiento y muerte y abrirnos la puerta al servicio devocional amoroso. La personificación de la misericordia de Srimati Radharani ... Estaba ahí y yo paralizada.

Bhaktivedanta Narayan Goswami Maharaja - Parikrama

Hasta que vino un vaisnava que años atrás me había apoyado para tomar Harinam y me ayudó a pasar. Tal como si Guru Maharaja lo hubiera mandado a animarme. El devoto me dio un asana y, al entrar, mi primera impresión fue que Srila Gurudeva era una murti, lleno de energía espiritual, grave, profundo. Por un instante lo confundí con una murti de Srila Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada y no dejaba de pensar que [tenemos acceso a] todo este proceso por la gracia de nuestro Prabhupada.

Gurudeva me miró una vez y yo durante el resto del programa no dejé de pensar en lo afortunada que era de poder estar ahí.

Al terminar la clase, Gurudeva salió por atrás y una devota me tomó de la mano y me dijo, "Este es el momento en que vas a poder interactuar con él".

Habían muchas personas y de alguna forma llegamos a estar justo en frente de Srila Gurudeva, yo seguía paralizada, simplemente no podía hablar. La devota me presentó con Srila Gurudeva, él miró y me dijo: "Finally you came" (finalmente viniste). Entre miles de discípulos y personas alrededor de él, de una u otra manera sabía que yo había orado por ese momento durante años y probablemente vidas. El acogía a todos y todas de la misma manera amorosa, compasiva. Tenía una relación diferente con cada uno de sus discípulos.

En los siguientes años tuve la oportunidad de tener otros darshanas con él y siempre fue inmensamente amoroso, recordaba a todos y hasta me preguntaba por hermanos y hermanas espirituales de Costa Rica.

Él sabía cuánto más lo necesitábamos.

Bhaktivedanta Narayan Goswami Maharaja

En otra ocasión, en [el festival de] Badger de 2010, estaba muy triste, pasando por una situación personal difícil y recuerdo que no me pude acercar mucho a Srila Gurudeva en esa ocasión. Sin embargo, el último día me determiné a llevarle flores y tener un darshan después de la caminata matutina. Así que desde temprano fui a recoger flores junto con otras hermanas espirituales y esperamos con ansias el carro que traía a Srila Gurudeva de su caminata. Ese día habían muchas personas, era el penúltimo día de festival. Todas las devotas querían tomar darshan e interactuar con él. Yo me quedé atrás, pensando que no iba poder [darle las flores], ya que solo teníamos unos minutos para verle mientras se bajaba del auto y entraba a su habitación, y eran tantas personas...

Pero él sabía que yo estaba ahí, miró atrás, entre todas sus discípulas y me preguntó: "How are you?" Movió su cabeza como diciéndome, "Qué tienes para mí" y le di todas las flores que había recolectado. Él las tomó y las puso dentro de su bolsa para la japa mala. Y sacó del bolsillo de su camisa muchas flores y se las dio a Sripad Bhaktivedanta Madhava Maharaja para que él me las pasara. Madhava Maharaja le dijo: "Ella es de Costa Rica". Guru Maharaja se acercó a mí y me dijo: "Dame también tu corazón y la tristeza se irá".

Estoy profundamente agradecida por haber tenido la oportunidad de encontrar a esta gran personalidad. Oro para que él nos acompañe siempre mientras cruzamos este océano de nacimiento y muerte, y nos acoja a sus pies.

Las glorias de Srila Gurudeva son incontables y desde mi humilde posición, sin ninguna calificación, dedico estas memorias al polvo de sus pies. Que él nos bendiga con fe, determinación y asociación con vaisnavas.

Niyya-lila pravista om vishnupada Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Goswami Maharaja Ki Jaya!

Aspirando al servicio de Sri Guru y Vaisnavas,

Manimati Devi Dasi.

Costa Rica.

Bhaktivedanta Narayan Goswami Maharaja

I offer my humble obeisances at the lotus feet of my beloved Gurudeva, Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja.

For some years I was unable to take physical darshan of Srila Gurudeva. However, in that period, despite speaking very little English, I managed to have communication via e-mail with him.

Until one good day I was able to travel to California, to the Venice temple. I had been praying for that moment for 4 years, and, at last, I was going to take Srila Gurudeva's darshan. I kept thinking about how many lifetimes I had been waiting for the opportunity to meet a pure Vaisnava.

Everyone entered the temple, the devotees who accompanied me from Costa Rica went inside. But I fell apart. I went into the room where the shoes were kept and I didn't have the strength to look inside.

I knew that a direct associate of Sri Sri Radha Krsna was there, so close, just a few steps away. That personality who is Sakshad Hari, who is an ocean of mercy, the only one who can take us out of the cycle of birth and death and open the door to loving devotional service. He who is the embodiment of Srimati Radharani's mercy ... He was there and I was paralysed.

Bhaktivedanta Narayan Goswami Maharaja

Then, a Vaisnava came, a person who years ago had supported me in taking Harinam, and he helped me go inside. Just as if Guru Maharaja had sent him to encourage me. The devotee gave me an asana and, upon entering, my first impression was that Srila Gurudeva was a murti, full of spiritual energy, grave, deep. For an instant I mistook him for a murti of Srila Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada; I kept thinking that [we have access to] this whole process by the grace of our Prabhupada.

Gurudeva looked at me once and for the rest of the program I kept thinking about how fortunate I was to be able to be there.

At the end of the class, Gurudeva left through the back entrance and a lady devotee took me by the hand and said, "This is the moment when you will be able to interact with him".

There were a lot of people and somehow we got to be right in front of Srila Gurudeva. I was still paralysed; I just couldn't speak. The lady devotee introduced me to Srila Gurudeva. He looked at me and said: "Finally you came". Even among thousands of disciples and people around him, in one way or another he knew that I had prayed for that moment for years and probably lifetimes. He welcomed everyone in the same loving, compassionate way. He had a different relationship with each of his disciples.

In the following years, I had the opportunity to have other darshanas with him and he was always immensely affectionate. He remembered everyone and he would even ask me about god brothers and god sisters from Costa Rica.

He knew when we needed him the most.

On another occasion, during Badger [festival] 2010, I was very sad, going through a difficult personal situation and I remember that I could not go very close to Srila Gurudeva during that time. However, on the last day, I was determined to bring him flowers and have darshan after his morning walk. So I went early in the morning to pick flowers along with other god sisters and then we went toward the car that brought Srila Gurudeva from his walk. That day there were many people, it was the penultimate day of the festival. All the lady devotees wanted to take darshan and interact with him. I stayed behind, thinking that I was not going to be able [to give the flowers], since we only had a few minutes to see him as he got out of the car and entered his room, and there were so many people...

But he knew I was there, he looked back among all his disciples and asked me: "How are you? He shook his head as if to tell me, "What do you have there for me?" and I gave him all the flowers I had collected. He took them and put them inside his japa mala bag. And he took out of his shirt pocket many flowers and gave them to Sripad Bhaktivedanta Madhava Maharaja for him to give them to me. Madhava Maharaja told him: "She is from Costa Rica." Guru Maharaja came over towards me and said: "Give me your heart too and the sadness will go away."

I am deeply grateful to have had the opportunity to meet this great personality. I pray that he will always accompany us as we cross this ocean of birth and death, and he will give us shelter at his feet.

Srila Gurudeva's glories are countless and from my humble position, bereft of qualification, I dedicate these memoirs to the dust of his feet. May he bless us with faith, determination and association with Vaisnavas.

Nitya-lila pravista om vishnupada Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Goswami Maharaja Ki Jaya!

Aspiring for the service of Sri Guru and Vaisnavas,

Bhaktivedanta Narayan Goswami Maharaja - Parikrama

Manimati Devi Dasi.

Costa Rica.
