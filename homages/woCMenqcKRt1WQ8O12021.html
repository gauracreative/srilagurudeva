Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Balabhadra das

United Kingdom, London

All Glories to Sri Sri Guru and Gaurang

I offer my humble obeisances at the lotus feet of my beloved Gurudeva, Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja. The following is my humble offering for his divine Centennial Vyasa-puja.

I am presenting a poem as my offering.
This is sung in the "ISKCON" Damodarastakam melody.

Poem - 1

Hopeless and Helpless

I have become hopeless and helpless, fallen and forlorn.
I have become hopeless and helpless, the darkness before dawn?
He said “Don’t become hopeless.”
And yet here I stand.
Hopeless and helpless and lost in this land.

“Be strong and chant!” With fire in his eyes.
My body was shaking, was it fear or surprise?
It was closer to terror; it was finally revealed.
Terror of the love in his heart he concealed.

So I ran and I ran, so so far away,
Swearing I would come back again someday.
But I am hopeless and helpless with so much to lose.
I need your mercy to accept what you choose.

For you choose love in your heart, for the purest divine.
Far beyond any conception of mine.
The riddles and rhymes of the scriptures They leave.
Locked in a treasure chest for which you have the key.

Yet still, hopeless, so helpless.
Your gift is so high.
You promised the fallen but for me you deny.
For my mind is so crazy, I have so much to fear.
What you have to offer. For me, tis nowhere near.

You must come to save me, to show me the way.
Since you have left me I’ve become blind and astray.
My internal connection is incredibly weak
I need you before me to whom I can speak?

To whom can I come to and show them my heart.
My heart that was broken and beaten by maya.
The heart that you stole and spirited away.
To manifest my true form in Goloka one day.

But I’m hopeless and helpless
Yet again I will try
But you must send me someone from the spiritual sky.
To bring back the Truth and correct the path.
So I can finally feel a connection at last.

Someone brilliant, brighter, clearer and more illuminating than countless suns.

More cooling than the bathing in countless rays of the Moon.

Able to bring under her control the Supreme Controller of all.

More beautiful than can ever be imagined.

Unfathomable, yet so unmistakable.

Someone who’s loving glance can never be matched.

Someone……….

Like you.

To my heart.

Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Maharaja.

Poem - 2

Beauty Spread My Wings

Your unfathomable, indescribable, unsurpassable Divine beauty is unconquerable.
If fills me with the power to spread my wings and fly.
It fills me with the joy to reach and soar beyond the sky.
Your unprecedented, unmatchable, unequivocal supreme beauty floods my mind.
Let Your beauty spread my wings.
Let me soar.
Beyond the stars.
To the realm where only that beauty fills the land.
To where my soul can relish the dance of the Divine.
Lost in eternal bliss.
Forever in Your arms.
Amen.

Poem - 3

Closer that the air I breathe.

I know that it’s true, but so hard to believe.
You are closer to me than the air that I breathe.
Lost in the wheel I keep turning around.
Can’t find my place, I’m totally bound.
As I sit here alone, thinking of you.
Wandering, wondering without a clue.
I chant the Name, but with countless offences.
Ego puffed up and consciousness senseless.
I’m lost and confused and totally helpless.
Breaking so many rules and regulations.
I see no way out of my hellish destination.
I prayed to Prabhupada “send me someone like you.”
You came in my life, like him through and through.
So now I beg, send someone again.
There is no other way for me to remain.
I will deviate, endlessly with no release.
Until I realize.
You are closer to me than the air that I breathe.

Poem -4

Janmasthami

A Thief of hearts, a Thief in the night.
Sri Krsna He comes, so keep your butter out of sight!

Subala, Sridhama sakha and all,
A wrestling match with Sri Krsna they brawl.

With mother Yasoda He's so innocent and sweet,
With her parental affection no other can compete.

Nanda Baba, with his lakhs of cows,
Thinks Sri Krsna is herding among the boughs.

But He wanders the forest, playing His flute,
Making the gopis separation acute.

On His Janmasthami, transcendental appearance,
I pray that these truths will come into coherence.

Poem - 5

Where have you gone

Where have you gone, my beautiful soul
I can't see you now and I'm all alone

I can't hear your voice, and here in my heart
You're absence it pains me it tears me apart

Suddenly I feel like crying, sitting here I feel like dying. I need you to help me get out of this maya.
Come and set my soul on fire. Kindle in me the desire to serve Radha Krsna there is nothing higher.

(Hare Krishna)

You were the one to bring light in my life
Now that you’re gone there's just trouble and strife

It takes strong steady faith for me to believe
That you're closer to me than the air that I breathe

(Hare Krishna)

You said never criticize and never complain
You’re always chanting the pure holy name

You roar like a lion so fearless and brave
No one and nothing could stand in your way

(Hare Krishna)

The personification of nectarean love
Sweet Yugala Kishore sent you down from above

As a maidservant of Radhika you came to show
Lifes greatest wealth and pure divine goal

(Hare Krishna)

I’m so fallen and lowly and crooked I know
So please pick me up, show me how to glow

Please hear my plea, I beg and I pray
Teach me to be just like you one day

(Hare Krishna)

Aspiring for Srila Gurudeva's causeless mercy,

Balabhadra Das
