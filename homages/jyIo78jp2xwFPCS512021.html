Srila Gurudeva Centennial 2021

Śrī Śrīmad Bhaktivedānta Nārāyana Gosvāmī Mahāraja  •  100th Anniversary

Gitanjali Dasi

India, Bangalore

gurave gauracandraya radhikayai tadalaye
krsnaya krsna-bhaktaya tad-bhaktaya namo-namah

All Glories to Sri Guru and Gauranga!

Well, I am an ignorant conditioned soul. I do not know how to glorify a pure devotee, a rupanuga acarya, who has touched almost every Gaudiya Vaisnava devotee’s heart in this world! I am not even a Vaisnava, neither can I ever say I am aspiring to be, as my current endeavours are pratikula to pure devotion. Still, by the order of seniors and the desire of Srila Maharaja, I am going to offer my puspanjali at his lotus feet. A fool like me can’t touch his glories, but like a mother is pleased even by hearing the broken words uttered by her little ones, I am also hopeful that my guru-varga will accept this offering at their lotus feet.

My siksa-guru as well as param-gurudeva, nitya-lila pravista om-visnupada astottara-sata Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja Ji, is jagad-guru. Whenever I read Sri Gurvastakam, I feel how each line of Sri Gurvastakam is cent percent applicable for Srila Parama Gurudeva. He is ocean of mercy for people like me who are always getting burnt out in samsara-davanala; he is expert in all kind of services to the Yugala-Kisora; tirelessly distributing pure bhakti; injecting the greed of Radha-dasyam in the heart of every listener/reader of his Harikatha, and he is always engaged in his seva at Nivrta-Nikunja of Sri Radha-Raman-Vihari Ji.

I was introduced to Gaudiya Vaisnavism in April 2011. Prior to that I used to visit ISKCON and Krsna was always my favourite since early childhood days. Thanks to Srimanta Sankardeva (believed to be an incarnation of Lord Siva and early preacher of Vaisnava dharma in Assam somewhere during the same time Sriman Mahaprabhu descended), I was always aware that Krsna is the Supreme Personality of Godhead (eko deva eko sevo / eko bina nahi keo: There is only one God, Krsna, and we should only serve him). However, I was totally ignorant of the significance of Srimati Radharani. I still remember the first day I visited the Gaudiya Matha in Bangalore (served under the guidance of pujyapada Bhaktivedanta Vaikhanas Maharaja); there were two big pictures of Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja Ji and Srila Bhaktivedanta Swami Maharaja Prabhupada, kept side by side in an alter in one corner. I was amazed at the very first sight! Those blue eyes were so sharp, so deep and so capable of stealing anyone’s heart whoever had just one glance at them! Maybe that’s the beauty of Srimati Radharani and Her maidservants. They can even make Krsna enchanted by Their beauty and make Him fall at Their lotus feet. What to say about Their lotus face or lotus eyes, even tip of Their veni (braid) is enough to make Krsna unconscious in divine love. So, what to say about a conditioned soul like me!! I was so much attracted to Srila Parama Gurudeva. When I asked someone about the picture, they told me, “He is our Gurudeva and he entered nitya-lila just few months back”. I was feeling low, how unfortunate I am that I was there in this world, young and grown up but I could never get his association!

So, I started associating with Srila Parama Gurudeva through his Harikatha (thanks to purebhakti.com, which played a major role here), his books and a few of his sincere followers. And thus Srimati Radharani came to my life!! I came to know Her, and ever since I have known Her, life became beautiful! Krsna is no longer the centre of attraction! Srila Parama Gurudeva taught us what our relationship with Krsna is! Krsna is Radharani’s and we love Krsna because He is the beloved of our Radharani. If Srila Parama Gurudeva would not have descended in this world, we would not have known what is Radha-dasyam, what is that unnata-ujjavala-rasa Sriman Mahprabhu came to give, what is so significant about Srila Rupa Gosvami and Srila Raghunatha Dasa Gosvami. He taught us how to actually pray to Sri Syamasundara:

syamasundara sikhanda-sekhara
smera-hasa murali-manohara
radhika-rasika mam krpa-nidhe
sva-priya-carana-kinkarim kuru
–From Sri Radha-Prarthana by Sri Vitthalacarya

Srila Parama Gurudeva taught us what is our prayojana, Radha-dasyam, and how intense this laulyam (desire) should be! He inspired us to be like the cataka paksi (bird), who drinks the water of only the svati constellation. The bird may even die out of thirst or due to the fall of thunder while awaiting for this auspicious moment! But his goal is fixed and he doesn’t compromise with anything else in life!

That’s Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja for us, the prayojan-acarya, the messenger of Sri Rupa-Raghunatha and forever our well-wisher! As he said, “I am closer to your heart than the air you breathe”, he is always inspiring us, guiding us towards our goal. I know how much he loves me, I feel that in every moment of my life but I am so ignorant and stubborn that, I am not saranagata. Those in our guru-varga are an ocean of mercy and guru-tattva is indeed one, but still I am foolish, eternally conditioned and not surrendered - always, hankering for labha-puja-pratistha!

Those in our guru-varga are the real ‘Visnu’ (who are present everywhere). Radharani and Her associates are present everywhere, Krsna needs to become Mahaprabhu to enter into the mood of Radharani but Radharani and Her maidservants can be everywhere, can enter in anyone’s mood, in the kunjas where Krsna also needs permission!

Such are the glories of Srila Parama Gurudeva and all in our rupanuga guru-varga!

guru, vaiṣṇava, bhagavan—tinera smarana
tinera smaraṇe haya vighna-vinasana
–From Sri Caitanya-caritamrita, Adi-lila 1.20-21, by Srila Krsnadasa Kaviraja Gosvami

May our mind, our rasana (tongue), every activity of our life always be engaged in glorification of Sri Guru-Vaisnava and Bhagavan.

As Srila Bhaktivinoda Thakura said, tomare bhuliya, abidya-piraya, pidita rasana mora (my tongue has become afflicted with the disease of ignorance).

He also said, taba nija-jana, kono mahajane pathaiya dile tumi (You have sent an exalted soul, one of Your own associates [to save me]). Srila Parama Gurudeva is nija-jana (intimate associate) of Srimati Radharani and I pray at his lotus feet and to akhanda-guru-tattva that a day will come in this life itself, when this ignorance will be wiped out by their unconditional mercy. I am weak and there is nothing else which can put me on the track of pure bhakti apart from their unconditional mercy!

Srila Parama Gurudeva Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja Ji ki jaya! Srila Guru Padapadma Ji ki jaya! Ananta-koti vaisnava-vrnda ki jaya! Sri Gaura-Nityananda Prabhu Ji ki jaya! Sri Yugala-Kisora Jiu ki jaya!

Gaura Premanande Hari Hari Bol! Hare Krsna!

Aspiring to be servant of Guru and Vaishnavas,

Gitanjali Dasi

(Siksa & Grand Disciple of Srila Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja; disciple of Sripad Bhaktivedanta Sridhara Maharaja)
