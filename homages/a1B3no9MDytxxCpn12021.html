献给施瑞拉帕让古如戴的祷文

我虔敬地千万次地顶拜我的施瑞拉古如戴瓦-噢姆维施努帕达 阿施投塔拉 沙塔 施瑞 帕达 佩玛南达帕布吉

我虔敬地千万次地顶拜我的施瑞拉帕让古如戴-尼提亚 丽拉

帕维施塔 噢姆 维施努帕达 阿施投塔拉 沙塔 施瑞 施瑞玛 巴克提维丹塔 拿拉央那 哥斯瓦米 玛哈拉杰

我虔敬地千万次地顶拜如帕努嘎古如瓦尔嘎各位超卓的灵性导师

我是一个渺小、堕落、卑微、懒惰、愚昧、软弱且毫无资格的弱小灵魂，但我却清晰地记得您的第一次达善是在2016年的元月，当我的耳边响起清晰、坚定、有力的玛哈曼陀时，我并不知道就是您。您是Bhakta-bandhav---众生之友，是为了拯救最堕落者而来，而我是所有最堕落者中最幸运的，得到了您的垂怜。

我曾望着照片中您坚定的眼睛泪如泉涌，心里感受着从您倾泻而出的仁慈之流而不知为什么，每当这时，我这个愚昧的人就会因一己的骄傲和阿纳塔，被迫离开这甜美的瞬间，然后回到我猪狗一样的生活。

我曾在《施瑞古如达善》中看到您蓝色的坚毅的眼睛，听到您狮子一般的教导和您的生活轨迹，他们象风向标一样引导着我的生活。我的眼、耳、口、心，因此而达到了它生命的巅峰。

我在去过的所有地方都会看到您庄严的画像，我会听到所有我见过的人讲述您超然的荣耀，我会在所有的灯仪结束时听到您神圣的名字，但我这个卑微且愚昧的污浊灵魂却只是可怜巴巴地跟在唱诵队伍的后面，而不知道如何去祈求来自您的恩慈。

现在，我扑倒在您的莲花足前，祈求生生世世紧握您和外士那瓦的莲花足，跟随您和外士那瓦的教导，但却因太肮脏而怕被遗弃。所以，我发出卑微的请求：请求您和外士那瓦施予仁慈，让我在这个伟大传系中的一个小角落中苟活。

一个卑微的灵魂

Translation: Puspanjali for Srila Param Gurudeva

I sincerely pay thousands and thousands obeisances to my gurudeva OM Visnupada Sri Sripad Premananda Prabhuji, also same obeisances to my Srila Param Gurudeva Nitya-lila pavista Om Visnupada astotara-sata Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Goswami Maharaja, all our rupanuga guru-varga.

I am an insignificant, fallen, lazy, ignorant, weak and unqualified soul. I still clearly remembered it was on Janurary 2016 I got your dasana first time in this life. When the clear, resolute and powerful mahamantra entered to my ears, I did not realize it was from you. You are Bhakta-bandhav-friend of all the living entities, you have come to this world to deliver the most fallen souls, among of them I am the most fortunate one and have become the recipient of your mercy.

Before when I gazed your picture my torrent tears streamed out, I experienced the flow of your mercy in my heart. But only after one moment, the nectarian mercy flow disappeared because of my pride and anarthas, then I went back to my pig-dog-like life.

I had seen your penetrating blue eyes from Sri Guru Darsana, I had heard your lion-like instructions and your abundant glories of your life. My eyes, ears, mouth and heart have attained the summit of their lives because of this.

I had wandered again and again in your red Samadhi mandira, your powerful mantra chanting resounded in my heart, I hankered to offer a little flower to you, but no courage because of my fallen and dirty nature, I was so afraid that it may pollute the dust of your lotus feet.

I saw your grave picture in everywhere I went, I heard your trancedental glories from everyone I met, I also heard your divine name when jaya-dhvani recited. But this insignificant and ignorant soul just followed behind crowds without knowing how to pray for your mercy.

Now I am falling at your lotus feet and praying may I tightly grasp the lotus feet of you and vasnavas and follow the instructions of you and vasnavas, please don’t give up this poor soul who is covered by so much pollutions. Therefore, I humbly request you, please, please bestow your mercy upon me, give me a place in this great line.

The insignificant soul.
